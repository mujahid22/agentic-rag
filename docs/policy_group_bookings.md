# Meridian Airlines — Group Booking Policy
**Policy Reference:** MA-BK-004
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** Groups of 10 or more passengers on the same itinerary

---

## 1. Group Definition

A group booking applies to **10 or more passengers** travelling together on the same origin, destination, and travel date. Each passenger in the group must be on the same PNR or a linked group PNR issued under a single Meridian group contract (e.g., MA-GRP-2025-0042).

Groups of 9 or fewer passengers are handled as individual bookings under standard fare rules.

---

## 2. How to Request a Group Quote

Group bookings are not available through the Meridian Airlines website or mobile app.

**Submit a Group Request:**
- Email: groupbookings@meridianair.com
- Phone: +1-800-MERIDIAN (select option 4 for Groups)
- Minimum advance notice: **21 days before departure** for domestic; **30 days** for international

Required information in the request:
- Number of passengers (adults, children, infants separately)
- Desired travel dates and flexibility window
- Origin and destination (or full itinerary)
- Contact name, company/organisation name, email, and phone
- Fare class preference

Meridian Airlines will respond with a group quote within **2 business days**.

---

## 3. Group Fares

Group fares are negotiated based on group size, travel dates, route, and advance booking period.

Group fares are typically **5–15% below published Economy Flex fares** for domestic routes and **8–20% below** for international routes. Business class group fares are available for groups of 20 or more.

Group fares are not combinable with promotional fares, Meridian Miles redemption discounts, or corporate account discounts.

---

## 4. Deposit and Payment Schedule

| Stage | Amount Due | Deadline |
|---|---|---|
| Booking confirmation | 20% of total group fare | Within 5 business days of quote acceptance |
| Passenger name submission | Names for all passengers | 21 days before departure (domestic); 30 days (international) |
| Final payment | Remaining 80% balance | 14 days before departure (domestic); 21 days (international) |

Failure to meet payment deadlines results in automatic cancellation of the group booking. The deposit is non-refundable upon cancellation. Group contract reference numbers follow the format: **MA-GRP-YYYY-NNNN** (e.g., MA-GRP-2025-0042).

---

## 5. Complimentary Seats

Meridian Airlines provides complimentary seats on the following scale:

| Group Size | Complimentary Seats |
|---|---|
| 10–24 passengers | 1 complimentary seat |
| 25–49 passengers | 2 complimentary seats |
| 50–99 passengers | 3 complimentary seats |
| 100+ passengers | 1 seat per 25 passengers |

Complimentary seats are in Economy class regardless of the fare class of the rest of the group, unless upgraded at cost.

---

## 6. Name Submission and Changes

- Passenger names must be submitted in the format: **LAST/FIRST MIDDLE** (all capitals)
- Names must be submitted by the deadline in Section 4
- Name changes after submission: USD 50 per change (up to 5% of group size at no charge)
- Complete passenger substitutions after name submission: USD 100 per substitution

---

## 7. Seat Allocation

Group passengers are seated together as a block where aircraft configuration permits. Meridian Airlines does not guarantee adjacent seating for all passengers in groups exceeding 30 people on wide-body aircraft. Specific seat requests should be submitted with the group contract.

---

## 8. Group Cancellations

| Cancellation Timing | Penalty |
|---|---|
| More than 30 days before departure | Loss of deposit (20%) |
| 15–30 days before departure | 50% of total group fare |
| 8–14 days before departure | 75% of total group fare |
| 7 days or fewer before departure | 100% of total group fare (no refund) |

Partial cancellations (reducing group size) are subject to re-pricing at the applicable fare for the remaining group size.

---

## 9. Group Baggage

Group bookings receive the standard baggage allowance per passenger based on the fare class of the group contract. There is no additional group baggage allowance. Excess baggage fees apply individually per passenger at standard rates (see MA-BG-003).
