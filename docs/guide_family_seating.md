# Meridian Airlines — Family Seating Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-FA-003
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** Families and groups travelling with children under 12

---

## 1. Meridian Family Seating Guarantee

Meridian Airlines guarantees that **children under 12** will be seated **next to at least one accompanying adult** at no extra charge, provided:
- All passengers are on the same PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92)
- The booking is made at least **48 hours** before departure
- The family is travelling on the same flight

If Meridian cannot seat the child next to an adult despite this guarantee, Meridian will upgrade the affected passengers (child + one adult) to available adjacent seats in a higher-category seat at no charge.

---

## 2. Seat Selection for Families

### 2.1 Recommended Approach
To maximise the chance of sitting together, families should **select seats at the time of booking** or as soon as possible via Manage Booking.

| Fare Class | Seat Selection Timing |
|---|---|
| Economy Saver | Middle seats free at booking; preferred/aisle seats purchased anytime |
| Economy Flex | All standard seats free at booking |
| Business | All Business seats free at booking |
| First | All First suites free at booking |

### 2.2 Best Seats for Families
- **Bulkhead row** (Row 14 on narrow-body, Row 20 on wide-body): extra floor space, bassinet hook points for infants, but no under-seat storage
- **3-seat rows** (aisle + middle + window): ideal for families of 3
- **Window pairs** (2-seat rows on wide-body): ideal for a parent and child, allows child to look out and not disturb others

---

## 3. Bassinet Seat Assignment

Families travelling with infants who have requested a bassinet (MA-BS-XXXXXX) are assigned a **bulkhead seat**. This seat cannot be changed once the bassinet is confirmed as the bassinet is fixed to the bulkhead.

Key points:
- Request bassinet early (see MA-FA-001 Section 4)
- Bulkhead rows are premium seats — they are assigned free of charge to bassinet-requesting passengers
- If no bassinet seat is available, the family may be assigned a standard seat and a bassinet may not be available in-flight

---

## 4. Priority Boarding for Families

Families travelling with children aged **under 5** are included in **Zone 4 boarding** (priority boarding), regardless of fare class. This allows time to get settled before general boarding begins.

To use family priority boarding:
- Present the family's boarding passes at the Zone 4 boarding call
- No additional purchase needed
- Priority boarding extends to the entire family group on the same PNR

---

## 5. Families with Multiple Bookings

If a family is on **two separate PNRs** (e.g., due to a split booking), Meridian cannot guarantee the seating guarantee. Passengers should:
1. Contact the Meridian Contact Centre (+1-800-MERIDIAN) to link the bookings and request adjacent seating
2. Do this as early as possible — at least **72 hours** before departure
3. Seat adjacency subject to availability when bookings are not on the same PNR

---

## 6. Aircraft Configuration Reference

| Aircraft Type | Row Configuration | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Narrow-body (A320 family) | 3-3 (Economy) | Good for families of 3 or 6 |
| Wide-body (A350, B787) | 3-4-3 (Economy) | Centre 4-seat rows ideal for families of 4 |
| Wide-body (B777) | 3-3-3 (Economy) | Window pairs on sides; 3-centre rows for families of 3 |

Aircraft type for your flight is shown at meridianair.com → Manage Booking → Flight Details → Aircraft Type.
