# Meridian Airlines — Meridian Miles Earning Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-MM-001
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All Meridian Miles members (account format: MA-XXXXXXXX)

---

## 1. Programme Overview

Meridian Miles is the frequent flyer programme of Meridian Airlines. Members earn miles for qualifying flights and partner activity. Miles are redeemed for award flights, upgrades, and partner rewards.

Enrolment: meridianair.com/meridiamiles or Meridian app → Join Meridian Miles. Free membership. Member number issued immediately (format: **MA-XXXXXXXX**, e.g., MA-88234410).

---

## 2. Earning Miles on Meridian Flights

Miles are earned based on the distance flown (great-circle miles) multiplied by a class-of-travel earning multiplier.

### 2.1 Earning Multipliers by Fare Class

| Fare Class | Earning Multiplier | Example: DXB–LHR (3,400 miles) |
|---|---|---|
| Economy Saver | 25% | 850 miles |
| Economy Flex | 100% | 3,400 miles |
| Business | 150% | 5,100 miles |
| First | 200% | 6,800 miles |

### 2.2 Bonus Miles for Elite Members

Elite members earn an additional status bonus on top of the base multiplier:

| Tier | Status Bonus | Total Multiplier (Economy Flex example) |
|---|---|---|
| Blue (base) | 0% | 100% |
| Silver | +25% | 125% |
| Gold | +50% | 150% |
| Platinum | +100% | 200% |

---

## 3. Minimum Miles per Sector

Regardless of distance or multiplier, the minimum miles credited per sector are:

| Fare Class | Minimum Miles |
|---|---|
| Economy Saver | 250 miles |
| Economy Flex | 500 miles |
| Business | 750 miles |
| First | 1,000 miles |

---

## 4. Non-Qualifying Flights (Miles Not Earned)

Miles are **not earned** on:
- Award tickets (redemption flights)
- Flights operated by non-partner airlines on a Meridian ticket number (rare)
- Infant tickets (lap infants)
- No-show tickets
- Promotional fares marked "NON-EARN" at time of booking

---

## 5. Earning on Partner Airlines

Meridian Miles members earn miles when flying on Meridian's partner airlines, subject to the partner's earning rates.

| Partner Type | Earning Rate |
|---|---|
| Full Alliance Partner | 50–100% of miles flown (varies by fare class and partner) |
| Codeshare Partner | 25–75% of miles flown |
| Interline Partner | Miles not earned |

Partner earning requires the Meridian Miles number (e.g., MA-88234410) to be entered in the booking with the partner airline. Retroactive claims for missing partner miles must be submitted within **6 months** of travel via meridianair.com/missingmiles.

---

## 6. Earning Miles on Non-Flight Activity

| Activity | Miles Earned |
|---|---|
| Hotel stays (Meridian hotel partners) | Up to 3 miles per USD spent |
| Car rental (Meridian car partners) | Up to 2 miles per USD spent |
| Meridian co-branded credit card spend | 1–3 miles per USD spent (card-dependent) |
| Shopping via Meridian Mall portal | 2–10 miles per USD spent |
| Dining at partner restaurants | 3 miles per USD spent |

---

## 7. Miles Expiry

Miles expire if the account has **no earning or redemption activity for 24 consecutive months**. Status miles (used for tier qualification) expire annually at the end of the programme year (31 December).

To check miles balance and expiry date: meridianair.com → My Miles → Account Summary, or Meridian app → Miles Balance.
