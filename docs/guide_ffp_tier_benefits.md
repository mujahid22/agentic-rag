# Meridian Airlines — Meridian Miles Tier Benefits Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-MM-003
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All Meridian Miles status members

---

## 1. Tier Structure and Qualification

Meridian Miles has four membership tiers. Tier status is based on **Status Miles** (qualifying miles) earned in a programme year (1 January – 31 December).

| Tier | Status Miles Required | Colour |
|---|---|---|
| Blue | 0 (base, automatic) | Blue |
| Silver | 25,000 Status Miles | Silver |
| Gold | 50,000 Status Miles | Gold |
| Platinum | 100,000 Status Miles | Platinum |

Status Miles are a subset of redeemable miles. Economy Saver fares earn Status Miles at 25% of distance flown. Economy Flex earns 100%, Business 150%, First 200%.

## 5. Tier Retention and Downgrade

Tiers are reviewed annually on 31 January based on the previous calendar year's Status Miles.

| Scenario | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Maintain tier threshold | Status renewed for another year |
| Fall below threshold by less than 25% | Downgrade by one tier |
| Fall below threshold by more than 25% | Downgrade to Blue |
| No activity in 24 months | Account suspended; tier lost |

Members who are downgraded receive a 90-day grace period to earn additional Status Miles and retain their tier.

---

## 6. Tier Card and Recognition

Upon reaching Silver, Gold, or Platinum status, a physical membership card is issued by post within 14 business days. The member card number matches the Meridian Miles account number (MA-XXXXXXXX) and can be used at all Meridian counters and partner locations for tier recognition.
