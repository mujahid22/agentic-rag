# Meridian Airlines — Travel Documents Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-CK-004
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All routes, all passengers

---

## 1. Responsibility for Travel Documents

**It is the passenger's sole responsibility** to hold all valid travel documents required for their journey, including visas, health certificates, and any other entry requirements. Meridian Airlines will not carry a passenger who lacks the required documentation and accepts no liability for denied entry at the destination or transit point.

---

## 2. Passport Requirements

### 2.1 International Flights
All passengers on Meridian international flights must hold a valid passport.

| Requirement | Standard Rule |
|---|---|
| Validity | Passport must be valid for at least **6 months beyond** the return travel date |
| Blank pages | At least **2 blank visa pages** required |
| Condition | Passport must be undamaged and machine-readable |

Exceptions: Some countries accept passports valid for 3 months beyond the travel date. Passengers should verify entry requirements for their specific destination.

### 2.2 Domestic Flights
For domestic flights within the same country, a government-issued national ID or driver's licence is accepted in lieu of a passport in most jurisdictions. Check local regulations for country-specific rules.

---

## 3. APIS (Advance Passenger Information System)

Many governments require airlines to submit passenger data before departure. Meridian Airlines collects APIS data during check-in:

- Full name (as per passport)
- Date of birth
- Nationality
- Passport number, issue country, expiry date
- Country of residence
- Travel document type

APIS data collected via the Meridian app or website at check-in. Passengers who do not provide APIS data may be denied boarding.

---

## 4. Visa Requirements

### 4.1 Meridian Disclaimer
Visa requirements change frequently. Meridian Airlines provides guidance as a convenience only. **Passengers must verify current requirements** with the relevant embassy, consulate, or official government immigration website.

### 4.2 Common Visa Categories

| Visa Type | Description |
|---|---|
| Tourist / Visitor Visa | Required for leisure travel to many countries |
| Transit Visa | Required for some passengers transiting through a country, even airside |
| Business Visa | Required for business activities in many jurisdictions |
| Electronic Travel Authorisation (eTA / ESTA / ETA) | Electronic pre-clearance required by US, Canada, Australia, UK |

### 4.3 Passengers Without Required Visa
Passengers denied boarding due to missing or invalid visa documentation are subject to Economy Saver no-show penalties or standard cancellation fees depending on fare class (see MA-CX-001 and MA-CX-002). Meridian Airlines may also be liable to return the passenger to their origin country at its own cost under airline carrier liability regulations.

---

## 5. Travel Health Documents

Some destinations require proof of vaccination or health screening. Current examples include yellow fever vaccination certificates for travel to/from certain African and South American countries.

Meridian Airlines checks health documentation requirements at the time of booking. If a health document requirement exists for a route, passengers are notified in the booking confirmation and at online check-in.

Health document requirements: healthtrav@meridianair.com.

---

## 6. Unaccompanied Minor Travel Documents

Unaccompanied minors (under 18 travelling alone on Meridian) must carry:
- Valid passport (international) or national ID (domestic)
- Unaccompanied Minor form (MA-UM-FORM) completed by the booking guardian
- Emergency contact details for both the sending and receiving adults

See MA-SS-002 for full Unaccompanied Minor policy.
