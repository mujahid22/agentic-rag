# Meridian Airlines — Business & First Class Cancellation Policy
**Policy Reference:** MA-CX-002
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** Business and First class fare classes

---

## 1. Business Class — Cancellation Rules

Business class tickets are **fully refundable** with the following fee structure:

### 1.1 Standard Business Cancellation Fees

| Cancellation Timing | Fee | Refund |
|---|---|---|
| More than 24 hours before departure | USD 0 | Full cash refund to original payment method |
| Within 24 hours of departure | USD 75 per person | Remainder as cash refund |
| After scheduled departure (no-show) | USD 150 per person | Remainder as Meridian travel credit (24 months) |

### 1.2 Meridian Miles Gold and Platinum Waiver
Meridian Miles Gold and Platinum members receive a **full waiver of all cancellation fees** on Business class tickets, including within 24 hours of departure. The waiver applies to the member's ticket only, not to companion tickets on the same booking.

### 1.3 Corporate Account Waiver
Passengers holding a confirmed Meridian corporate account fare (fare code beginning with **MA-CN**) are exempt from all cancellation fees regardless of timing.

---

## 2. First Class — Cancellation Rules

First class tickets carry the most flexible cancellation terms.

### 2.1 First Class Cancellation Fees

| Cancellation Timing | Fee | Refund |
|---|---|---|
| Any time before scheduled departure | USD 0 | Full cash refund to original payment method |
| After scheduled departure (no-show) | USD 0 | Full cash refund to original payment method |

First class is unconditionally refundable. There are no cancellation fees under any circumstances.

### 2.2 First Class Refund Processing
Full cash refunds are processed within:
- Credit/debit card: **5–7 business days**
- Meridian Wallet: **24 hours**
- Bank transfer: **7–10 business days**

---

## 3. How to Cancel

### 3.1 Self-Service (Recommended)
- **Meridian Website / App:** Manage Booking → Enter PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) and last name → Select "Cancel Booking"
- Available 24/7, no service fee

### 3.2 Contact Centre
- Phone: +1-800-MERIDIAN (select option 2 for cancellations)
- No service fee for Business and First class cancellations
- Available 24/7

### 3.3 Airport Ticket Office
- Available up to gate close time for same-day departures

---

## 4. Involuntary Cancellation by Meridian Airlines

If Meridian Airlines cancels a Business or First class flight:
- Full cash refund is processed automatically within **72 hours** without requiring passenger action
- Alternative rebooking on the next available Meridian flight is offered at no charge
- If the alternative involves a lower cabin class, the fare difference is refunded immediately
- Hotel accommodation is provided if the delay requires an overnight stay (see MA-DC-002)

---

## 5. Partial Ticket Cancellations

### 5.1 Return Journey (One Leg)
Business and First class passengers may cancel either leg of a return journey independently without affecting the other leg. The remaining leg retains its original fare and conditions.

### 5.2 Multi-Segment Itineraries
Each segment of a multi-segment itinerary can be cancelled independently. The applicable fee is based on the time remaining before that specific segment's departure.

---

## 6. Award Tickets Cancelled in Business/First

Meridian Miles award tickets booked in Business or First class:
- Miles are re-credited to the member's account (e.g., MA-88234410) within **72 hours**
- Award reinstatement fee: USD 50 per ticket (waived for Gold and Platinum members)
- Taxes paid at time of award booking are refunded per standard timelines
