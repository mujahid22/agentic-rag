# Meridian Airlines — Lounge Day Pass Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-AN-002
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** Economy passengers on Meridian Airlines flights departing from Meridian hub airports

---

## 1. Meridian Horizon Lounge — Day Pass

The Meridian Horizon Lounge is accessible to Business class passengers and Meridian Miles Gold/Platinum members by default. Economy passengers may purchase a day pass for a single-visit lounge access.

### 1.1 Day Pass Pricing

| Purchase Channel | Price per Person |
|---|---|
| Pre-purchase online (48+ hours before) | USD 45 |
| Pre-purchase online (under 48 hours) | USD 55 |
| Airport lounge reception | USD 65 |
| Meridian app | USD 50 |

### 1.2 Day Pass Inclusions
- Unlimited food and non-alcoholic beverages
- Alcoholic beverages (beer, wine, spirits)
- High-speed Wi-Fi
- Charging stations
- Shower facilities (subject to availability; first come first served)
- Printing and business centre access
- Access from 3 hours before departure to final boarding call

### 1.3 Lounge Locations (Day Pass Valid)
- **Dubai (DXB):** Meridian Horizon Lounge, Terminal 3, Gates B1–B30
- **London (LHR):** Meridian Horizon Lounge, Terminal 4, airside
- **New York (JFK):** Meridian Horizon Lounge, Terminal 8, post-security

### 1.4 Day Pass Reference
Day pass reference format: **MA-LP-XXXXXX** (e.g., MA-LP-XKP192). Present at lounge reception with boarding pass and day pass QR code.

---

## 2. Meridian First Lounge — Day Pass

The Meridian First Lounge is a premium private lounge available to First class passengers and Meridian Miles Platinum members. Day passes for Economy and Business passengers:

| Purchase Channel | Price per Person |
|---|---|
| Pre-purchase online | USD 120 |
| Airport lounge reception | USD 150 |

First Lounge is available at **DXB and LHR only**. No First Lounge at JFK currently.

First Lounge extras (vs Horizon Lounge):
- Private dining with à la carte menu
- Spa treatments (30-minute slot, bookable at lounge; additional fee applies)
- Quiet zones and private pods
- Personal concierge service

---

## 3. Guest Policy

Day pass holders may bring guests into the Meridian Horizon Lounge:

| Guest Fee | Price |
|---|---|
| Adult guest | USD 35 per guest |
| Child (2–11 years) | USD 20 per guest |
| Infant (under 2) | Free |

Maximum 2 guests per day pass holder. Guests must be travelling on the same Meridian Airlines flight as the day pass holder.

---

## 4. Non-Eligible Passengers

The Meridian Horizon Lounge Day Pass is **not available** for passengers:
- Transiting (not departing on a Meridian flight from that airport)
- On flights operated by partner airlines under a Meridian codeshare
- Arriving passengers (departing passengers only)

---

## 5. How to Purchase

1. **Online:** meridianair.com → Manage Booking → Enter PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) → Lounge Pass
2. **App:** My Bookings → Select flight → Add Lounge Access
3. **Airport:** Meridian Horizon Lounge reception desk (subject to capacity)

Day passes are non-refundable within 24 hours of the flight. Full refund if cancelled more than 24 hours before the flight.
