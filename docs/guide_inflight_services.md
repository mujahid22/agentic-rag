# Meridian Airlines — In-Flight Services Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-AN-003
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All Meridian Airlines flights

---

## 1. Wi-Fi Service

### 1.1 Availability
Meridian Airlines Wi-Fi (branded **MeridianConnect**) is available on all wide-body international aircraft and on domestic narrow-body aircraft operating routes over 2 hours.

### 1.2 Wi-Fi Plans

| Plan | Speed | Price | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Chat & Browse | 5 Mbps | USD 8 per flight | Messaging, email, light browsing |
| Stream & Work | 15 Mbps | USD 18 per flight | Video calls, streaming, large files |
| Full Flight Unlimited | 15 Mbps | USD 25 per flight | Heavy users, all-day connectivity |
| Hourly Pass | 10 Mbps | USD 6 per hour | Short-use top-up |

Wi-Fi purchase is available:
- Pre-flight: meridianair.com → Manage Booking → Enter PNR (MA-XKPL92) → Add Wi-Fi
- Onboard: connect to the **MeridianConnect** network on your device and purchase at the captive portal
- Business class passengers: 1-hour complimentary Wi-Fi included; full flight at USD 15
- First class passengers: complimentary Wi-Fi for the full flight

## 2. In-Flight Entertainment (IFE)

### 2.1 Seatback Screens
All Meridian Airlines wide-body aircraft are equipped with personal HD seatback touchscreens. Content includes:
- 300+ movies (latest releases and classics)
- 500+ hours of TV series
- Music: 2,000+ albums across all genres
- Podcasts and audiobooks
- Moving map and flight information display
- Kids' entertainment zone

### 2.2 Streaming on Personal Device
On Meridian narrow-body aircraft without seatback screens:
- Stream content via the **Meridian IFE App** (iOS and Android)
- Connect to the **MeridianStream** onboard network (free — no Wi-Fi subscription needed)
- Content library: 150+ movies, 200+ TV episodes
- Headphone jack on armrest (3.5 mm) on all wide-body aircraft

### 2.3 Headphones
- Economy: earbuds provided on flights over 3 hours at no charge
- Business: noise-cancelling headphones provided
- First: premium noise-cancelling headphones provided (to keep)

## 3. In-Flight Retail

The Meridian Sky Shop is available on all international flights. Products include:
- Duty-free perfume and cosmetics
- Spirits and tobacco
- Confectionery and snacks
- Meridian Airlines branded merchandise
- Electronics (selected flights)

Payment: cash (USD, GBP, EUR, AED), Visa, Mastercard, Meridian Wallet.
Sky Shop catalogue: available in the seatback pocket and on the IFE screen.

Meridian Miles members earn **2 miles per USD spent** in the Sky Shop.

---

## 4. Meal Pre-Order Service

On Economy Saver flights over 5 hours where a standard meal is included, passengers may pre-order a premium meal for an additional fee:

| Premium Meal Upgrade | Fee |
|---|---|
| Premium Economy meal (3-course) | USD 20 per person |
| Chef's Selection meal | USD 35 per person |

Pre-order deadline: **24 hours before departure** via meridianair.com or Meridian app.
