# Meridian Airlines — Unaccompanied Minor (UM) Policy
**Policy Reference:** MA-SS-002
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** Children travelling without an accompanying adult

---

## 1. Age Eligibility

| Age | UM Service |
|---|---|
| Under 5 years | Not accepted as unaccompanied minor on any Meridian flight |
| 5–11 years | Mandatory UM service (compulsory, no opt-out) |
| 12–15 years | Optional UM service (parent/guardian may request) |
| 16–17 years | Travel as adult; UM service not available |

---

## 2. UM Service Fee

The Unaccompanied Minor service fee is charged per sector (one way) and is separate from the air ticket fare.

| Route Type | UM Fee per Sector |
|---|---|
| Domestic | USD 60 per sector |
| International Short-Haul | USD 80 per sector |
| International Long-Haul | USD 100 per sector |

The UM fee is non-refundable once the booking is confirmed, unless Meridian Airlines cancels the flight.

---

## 3. Booking a UM

UM travel cannot be booked online. All UM bookings must be made via:
- Meridian Contact Centre: +1-800-MERIDIAN
- Airport Ticket Office

Required at booking:
- Child's full name, date of birth, nationality
- Sending adult's name, relationship, contact phone number
- Receiving adult's name, relationship, contact phone number, address
- Confirmation that the receiving adult will be at the destination airport

UM booking reference format: **MA-UM-XXXXXX** (e.g., MA-UM-XKP192).

---

## 4. Restricted Routes for UMs

Unaccompanied Minors are **not accepted** on:
- Flights with connections where the connection time is less than 2 hours
- Flights arriving after 22:00 local time at the destination (except where a direct supervised handoff is pre-arranged)
- The last flight of the day on routes with no alternative if the flight is cancelled
- Codeshare flights operated by partner airlines (Meridian-operated only)

---

## 5. Airport Handling

### 5.1 Sending Airport
- Sending adult must check the child in at the **UM check-in desk** (not self-service)
- Sending adult must remain at the airport until the flight has departed
- Sending adult must present valid photo ID
- UM is escorted to the gate and onto the aircraft by Meridian ground staff

### 5.2 Onboard
- Flight crew is notified of the UM's presence
- UM is assigned a seat near the front of the cabin (bulkhead where possible)
- Crew monitors the child throughout the flight

### 5.3 Destination Airport
- UM is escorted off the aircraft by Meridian staff
- UM is held in the Meridian UM lounge until the receiving adult arrives
- Receiving adult must present valid photo ID and a copy of the UM booking confirmation (MA-UM-XXXXXX)
- If the receiving adult does not appear within **3 hours**, local child welfare authorities may be contacted

---

## 6. Documents Required

| Document | Required For |
|---|---|
| Completed MA-UM-FORM | All UM journeys |
| Child's passport or ID | Domestic and international |
| Sending adult's photo ID | Sending airport check-in |
| Receiving adult's photo ID | Destination airport collection |

The MA-UM-FORM is available at meridianair.com/umform or at any Meridian Ticket Office.

---

## 7. Connections

UM with connecting Meridian flights on the same PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) will be escorted between gates by Meridian staff during the layover. The minimum connection time for UM travel is **2 hours**. UM cannot travel on separate PNRs for connecting segments.
