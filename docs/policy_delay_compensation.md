# Meridian Airlines — Flight Delay Compensation Policy
**Policy Reference:** MA-DC-001
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All Meridian Airlines-operated flights (prefix MA-)

---

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to delays caused by Meridian Airlines operations, including technical issues, crew availability, and aircraft substitution. It does **not** apply to delays caused by weather, air traffic control (ATC), strikes outside Meridian's control, or other extraordinary circumstances (see MA-DC-004).

---

## 2. Delay Compensation and Entitlements

### 2.1 Meals and Refreshments

| Delay Duration | Entitlement |
|---|---|
| 2 hours or more | Meal voucher: USD 15 per person (redeemable at airport restaurants) |
| 4 hours or more | Meal voucher: USD 30 per person + free phone calls / internet access (30 minutes) |
| 6 hours or more | Meal voucher: USD 50 per person + free lounge access (economy passengers) |

Meal vouchers are issued at the departure gate or check-in area by Meridian ground staff. Voucher format: **MA-MV-XXXXXX** (e.g., MA-MV-XKP192). Vouchers expire at end of day of issue.

### 2.2 Cash Compensation

Cash compensation applies only to delays that are within Meridian's control and where the passenger was not rebooked to an alternative flight arriving within 2 hours of the original scheduled arrival.

| Delay at Destination | Route Type | Cash Compensation |
|---|---|---|
| 3–4 hours | Domestic | USD 100 per person |
| 3–4 hours | International | USD 150 per person |
| 4–6 hours | Domestic | USD 200 per person |
| 4–6 hours | International | USD 300 per person |
| Over 6 hours | Domestic | USD 300 per person |
| Over 6 hours | International | USD 500 per person |

Cash compensation is paid via the original payment method or as a Meridian travel credit (MA-TC-XXXXXX). Passengers may choose their preferred form.

### 2.3 Hotel Accommodation (Overnight Delays)

If a delay requires an overnight stay:
- Hotel accommodation arranged and paid by Meridian Airlines at a 3-star or above property
- Airport transfers provided
- Meal allowance: USD 50 per person per day
- Passengers must request accommodation at the Meridian service desk before leaving the airport

---

## 3. Right to Rebook

Passengers affected by a delay of 3 hours or more at the destination may choose to:
- Accept the delay and receive the entitlements in Section 2, **or**
- Rebook to the next available Meridian flight to the destination at no charge, **or**
- Cancel the booking for a full refund (including non-refundable fares) if the delay is 5 hours or more

Rebooking requests: meridianair.com → Manage Booking → Enter PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) → Rebooking Options, or Contact Centre +1-800-MERIDIAN.

---

## 4. Claiming Compensation

Compensation must be claimed within **12 months** of the delayed flight.

1. Submit claim at: meridianair.com/delayclaim
2. Required: PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92), flight number (e.g., MA-204), travel date, original scheduled and actual departure/arrival times
3. Processing time: 15–20 business days
4. Contact: delayclaims@meridianair.com

---

## 5. Connecting Flights Missed Due to Delay

If a Meridian-caused delay results in a missed connection on the same Meridian PNR:
- Rebooking to the next available Meridian connection at no charge
- If no Meridian connection within 3 hours: partner airline rebooking arranged
- If arrival at final destination delayed by 3+ hours due to missed connection: full compensation under Section 2 applies
