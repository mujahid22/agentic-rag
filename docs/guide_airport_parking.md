# Meridian Airlines — Airport Parking Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-AP-003
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** Passengers driving to/from Meridian hub airports

---

## 1. Overview

Meridian Airlines has partnered with airport parking operators at its three hub airports to offer discounted parking rates for Meridian passengers.

Parking is not operated by Meridian Airlines. Discounts are facilitated through the Meridian Parking Partner Programme. Meridian Airlines accepts no liability for vehicle damage, theft, or parking disputes.

---

## 2. Dubai (DXB) — Parking Partners

| Facility | Type | Distance | Standard Rate | Meridian Discount Rate |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| DXB Terminal 3 Short Stay | Multi-storey | Attached | AED 20/hour | AED 15/hour (25% off) |
| DXB Long Stay Park 1 | Open lot | 10 min shuttle | AED 50/day | AED 38/day (25% off) |
| DXB Long Stay Park 2 | Covered | 15 min shuttle | AED 70/day | AED 50/day (28% off) |
| Meridian VIP Parking | Covered reserved | 5 min shuttle | AED 120/day | AED 90/day (Gold/Platinum only) |

How to access discount: enter promo code **MA-PARK-DXB** at DXB parking portal (parking.dubaiairports.ae) or present Meridian boarding pass at the payment machine.

## 5. Meridian Miles — Miles for Parking

Meridian Miles members earn **1 Meridian Mile per USD 2 spent** at Meridian Parking Partner facilities. Provide Meridian Miles account number (e.g., MA-88234410) at time of parking payment.

Retroactive miles claims for parking: submit within **30 days** of parking date at meridianair.com/missingmiles.

---

## 6. Gold and Platinum Parking Benefits

| Tier | Additional Benefit |
|---|---|
| Gold | Extra 5% discount on top of standard Meridian rate at all partner facilities |
| Platinum | Access to Meridian VIP Parking at DXB; preferred spots at LHR and JFK partner lots |

Gold and Platinum benefits require Meridian Miles card or account number at time of booking.

---

## 7. Booking in Advance

Pre-booking parking is strongly recommended, especially during peak periods (school holidays, public holidays). Discounted rates may not be available for walk-in bookings. Book at:
- meridianair.com/parking
- Meridian app → My Trip → Add Parking

Parking booking reference format: **MA-PKG-XXXXXX** (e.g., MA-PKG-XKP192).
