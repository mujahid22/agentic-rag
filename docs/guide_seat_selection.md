# Meridian Airlines — Seat Selection Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-CK-003
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All fare classes, all routes

---

## 1. Seat Selection by Fare Class

| Fare Class | Free Seat Selection | Paid Seat Selection | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Economy Saver | Middle seats only (free) | Window/aisle: USD 15–35 | Preferred/extra legroom: USD 25–65 |
| Economy Flex | Any standard seat (free) | Preferred/extra legroom: USD 20–50 | Exit row: USD 30–70 |
| Business | Any Business seat (free) | N/A | Lie-flat seat assignment included |
| First | Any First suite (free) | N/A | Private suite included |

Seat selection can be made at time of booking or via Manage Booking at meridianair.com. Seat selection is confirmed in the booking PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92).

---

## 2. Seat Categories (Economy)

### 2.1 Standard Seats
- Middle seats in rows 20–40 (narrow-body) or rows 30–50 (wide-body)
- 78 cm (31 inch) seat pitch

### 2.2 Preferred Seats
- All window and aisle seats
- First 10 rows of Economy cabin (faster deplaning)
- 78 cm (31 inch) seat pitch
- Fee: USD 15–35 per sector

### 2.3 Extra Legroom Seats
- Rows 14–16 on narrow-body aircraft
- Rows 20–22 on wide-body aircraft
- 91 cm (36 inch) seat pitch
- Fee: USD 25–65 per sector

### 2.4 Exit Row Seats
- Rows adjacent to emergency exits
- 107 cm (42 inch) seat pitch (some rows)
- Fee: USD 30–70 per sector
- Passengers must be 15+ years old, physically able to operate the exit, and willing to assist in an emergency. Flight attendants will brief exit row passengers separately.

---

## 3. Pre-Assigned Seats for Special Needs

The following passengers receive free pre-assigned seats regardless of fare class:
- Unaccompanied minors: bulkhead seat
- Passengers travelling with infants: bassinet row (bulkhead) — subject to availability; request via specialassistance@meridianair.com
- Passengers with medical needs: aisle seat guaranteed
- Passengers requiring wheelchair assistance: aisle seat at front of cabin

---

## 4. Family and Group Seating

### 4.1 Children Under 12
Meridian Airlines guarantees that children under 12 are seated adjacent to at least one accompanying adult at no extra charge, regardless of fare class. This guarantee applies when booking is made at least **48 hours before departure** on a single PNR.

### 4.2 Groups
Groups of 6 or more on the same PNR may request block seating via groupbookings@meridianair.com when the booking is made. Block seating is subject to availability but prioritised for group bookings.

## 5. Seat Change Policy

| Timing | Economy Saver | Economy Flex | Business / First |
|---|---|---|---|
| Before online check-in opens | Paid (USD 15–35) | Free | Free |
| During online check-in | Free (standard seats) | Free | Free |
| At airport | Free if available | Free | Free |
| After boarding pass issued | Not permitted | Contact Centre | Gate agent |

---

## 6. Seat Upgrades

Selecting a higher-category seat (e.g., standard → preferred) does not constitute a cabin upgrade. Cabin class upgrades are handled under MA-FK-003. Seat selection fees are non-refundable unless Meridian Airlines changes the aircraft type resulting in a seat reassignment.
