# Meridian Airlines — Economy Class Cancellation Policy
**Policy Reference:** MA-CX-001
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** Economy Saver and Economy Flex fare classes

---

## 1. Economy Saver — Cancellation Rules

Economy Saver is a **non-refundable** fare. The following rules apply:

### 1.1 Cancellation Fees

| Cancellation Timing | Fee | Refund |
|---|---|---|
| More than 7 days before departure | USD 100 per person | Remainder as Meridian travel credit |
| 2–7 days before departure | USD 150 per person | Remainder as Meridian travel credit |
| Within 48 hours of departure | USD 200 per person | Remainder as Meridian travel credit |
| After departure (no-show) | Full fare forfeited | No refund, no credit |

Travel credits issued for Economy Saver cancellations:
- Valid for **12 months** from the original ticket issue date
- Applicable to Meridian Airlines flights only
- Non-transferable (must be used by the original passenger)
- Credit code format: **MA-TC-XXXXXX** (e.g., MA-TC-XKP192)

### 1.2 24-Hour Risk-Free Cancellation
Bookings made at least **7 days before departure** may be cancelled within **24 hours of booking** for a full refund to the original payment method, regardless of fare class. This window is available once per booking.

---

## 2. Economy Flex — Cancellation Rules

Economy Flex is a **partially refundable** fare.

### 2.1 Cancellation Fees

| Cancellation Timing | Fee | Refund |
|---|---|---|
| More than 7 days before departure | USD 50 per person | Cash refund to original payment method |
| 2–7 days before departure | USD 75 per person | Cash refund to original payment method |
| Within 48 hours of departure | USD 100 per person | Cash refund to original payment method |
| After departure (no-show) | USD 150 per person | Remainder as Meridian travel credit |

### 2.2 Refund Processing Time
Cash refunds for Economy Flex cancellations are processed within:
- Credit/debit card: **7–10 business days**
- Meridian Wallet: **24–48 hours**
- Bank transfer: **10–14 business days**

---

## 3. How to Cancel

Cancellations can be submitted through:

1. **Meridian Website / App:** Manage Booking → Enter PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) and last name → Select "Cancel Booking"
2. **Contact Centre:** +1-800-MERIDIAN (agent fee of USD 25 applies)
3. **Airport Ticket Office:** Prior to check-in closing time only

---

## 4. Partial Cancellations (One Leg of a Return)

For return tickets, passengers may cancel one leg independently:

| Scenario | Economy Saver | Economy Flex |
|---|---|---|
| Cancel outbound only | Permitted, fees apply | Permitted, fees apply |
| Cancel return only | Permitted, fees apply | Permitted, fees apply |
| Cancel both legs | Treated as full cancellation | Treated as full cancellation |

Cancelling one leg does not entitle the passenger to a refund on the remaining leg.

---

## 5. Taxes and Government Fees

Unused government taxes and airport fees are **fully refundable** on all Economy fares regardless of cancellation timing, unless the tax is non-refundable by regulation. Airline-imposed charges (fuel surcharge, carrier surcharge) are not refundable on Economy Saver.

---

## 6. Promotional and Sale Fares

Special promotional fares (marked "PROMO" or "SALE" at time of booking) are **non-cancellable and non-refundable** under any circumstances, including the 24-hour risk-free window. Meridian travel credits are not issued on promotional fares. Passengers should review fare conditions carefully before purchasing.
