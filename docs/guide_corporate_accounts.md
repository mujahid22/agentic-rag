# Meridian Airlines — Corporate Account Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-CO-001
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** Organisations with a Meridian Airlines corporate travel agreement

---

## 1. What is a Meridian Corporate Account?

A Meridian Corporate Account is a commercial agreement between Meridian Airlines and an organisation that provides:
- Negotiated discount fares on qualifying routes
- Enhanced flexibility on ticket changes and cancellations
- Centralised billing and invoicing
- Dedicated account management

Corporate accounts are available to organisations with a minimum annual Meridian Airlines spend of **USD 50,000**.

---

## 2. Corporate Account Number Format

Upon signing a corporate agreement, the organisation is issued a **Corporate Account Number**:
- Format: **MA-CORP-XXXXXXXX** (e.g., MA-CORP-00128374)
- This number must be entered at booking to access negotiated fares
- Fares booked under the corporate account carry fare codes beginning with **MA-CN**

---

## 3. Benefits of a Meridian Corporate Account

### 3.1 Fare Discounts
| Volume Tier | Discount (Economy) | Discount (Business) |
|---|---|---|
| USD 50K–150K/year | 8% off published fares | 5% off published fares |
| USD 150K–500K/year | 12% off published fares | 8% off published fares |
| Over USD 500K/year | 15–20% off (negotiated) | 12–15% off (negotiated) |

Discounts apply to Economy Flex and Business class only. Economy Saver and First class are excluded from standard corporate discounts.

### 3.2 Enhanced Flexibility
- Free date/time changes on all fare classes (including Economy Saver) for MA-CN fares
- No cancellation fees on any MA-CN fare
- Corporate fares are fully refundable regardless of original fare conditions

### 3.3 Centralised Billing
- Monthly consolidated invoice in PDF and CSV format
- Multiple cost centres supported (each cost centre receives its own sub-account)
- VAT/GST invoices available for applicable jurisdictions
- Online reporting dashboard: meridianair.com/corporate/dashboard

### 3.4 Dedicated Account Manager
Corporate accounts with annual spend over USD 150,000 receive a dedicated Meridian Account Manager:
- Single point of contact for all account queries
- Quarterly business reviews
- Early access to route and fare announcements

---

## 4. How to Apply

Submit a corporate account application to:
- Email: corporate@meridianair.com
- Online: meridianair.com/corporate/apply

Required information:
- Organisation legal name and registration number
- Primary contact name, email, phone
- Estimated annual travel spend (flights only)
- Routes of most frequent travel
- Preferred invoicing and billing arrangements

Processing time: **5–10 business days**. A draft agreement is presented for review before activation.

---

## 5. Booking Under a Corporate Account

- Include the Corporate Account Number (MA-CORP-XXXXXXXX) at the time of booking via the Meridian website, app, or TMC (Travel Management Company)
- Confirm the fare code is **MA-CN** on the booking confirmation
- All bookings under the account are tracked in the corporate dashboard

---

## 6. Unused Ticket Management

Corporate accounts receive access to the **Meridian Ticket Exchange** programme:
- Unused MA-CN tickets (fully unused) can be exchanged for a Meridian travel credit (MA-TC-XXXXXX) valid for 24 months
- Credits are pooled at the account level (not passenger level) — any traveller in the organisation can use them
- No fee for ticket exchange under the corporate account
