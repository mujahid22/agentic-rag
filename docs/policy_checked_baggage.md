# Meridian Airlines — Checked Baggage Policy
**Policy Reference:** MA-BG-002
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All fare classes, all routes

---

## 1. Free Checked Baggage Allowance

### 1.1 Domestic Routes

| Fare Class | Number of Bags | Max Weight per Bag | Max Dimensions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Economy Saver | 0 (no free bag) | — | — |
| Economy Flex | 1 bag | 23 kg | 158 cm combined |
| Business | 2 bags | 32 kg each | 158 cm combined |
| First | 3 bags | 32 kg each | 158 cm combined |

### 1.2 International Short-Haul (under 5 hours)

| Fare Class | Number of Bags | Max Weight per Bag | Max Dimensions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Economy Saver | 1 bag | 23 kg | 158 cm combined |
| Economy Flex | 1 bag | 23 kg | 158 cm combined |
| Business | 2 bags | 32 kg each | 158 cm combined |
| First | 3 bags | 32 kg each | 158 cm combined |

### 1.3 International Long-Haul (5+ hours)

| Fare Class | Number of Bags | Max Weight per Bag | Max Dimensions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Economy Saver | 1 bag | 23 kg | 158 cm combined |
| Economy Flex | 2 bags | 23 kg each | 158 cm combined |
| Business | 2 bags | 32 kg each | 158 cm combined |
| First | 3 bags | 32 kg each | 158 cm combined |

---

## 2. Oversize Bags

A bag is **oversize** if combined dimensions exceed 158 cm but are within 292 cm.

| Route Type | Oversize Fee |
|---|---|
| Domestic | USD 75 per bag |
| International Short-Haul | USD 100 per bag |
| International Long-Haul | USD 150 per bag |

Bags exceeding 292 cm combined must be shipped as cargo.

---

## 3. Overweight Bags

| Weight Band | Domestic Fee | International Fee |
|---|---|---|
| 24–32 kg | USD 50 per bag | USD 75 per bag |
| 33–45 kg | USD 100 per bag | USD 150 per bag |
| Over 45 kg | Not accepted | Not accepted |

---

## 4. Meridian Miles Tier Baggage Benefits

| Tier | Additional Benefit |
|---|---|
| Silver | +1 bag on all routes; weight limit increased to 28 kg per bag |
| Gold | +2 bags on all routes; weight limit increased to 32 kg per bag |
| Platinum | +3 bags on all routes; 32 kg per bag; oversize fee waived |

Benefits apply to the status member's ticket only. Quote your Meridian Miles number (e.g., MA-88234410) at check-in.

---

## 5. Connecting Flights

For itineraries on a single Meridian PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92), checked baggage is transferred automatically to the final destination. The baggage allowance of the **first departing flight's fare class** applies throughout.

---

## 6. Delayed, Damaged, and Lost Baggage

- **Delayed:** File a Property Irregularity Report (PIR) immediately at the Meridian baggage desk. Reference number format: **MA-DXB-99999** (airline code + 3-letter airport + 5 digits).
- **Damaged:** Report at the Meridian baggage desk before leaving the airport.
- **Lost:** Declared lost after 21 days. Compensation up to USD 1,700 per passenger per IATA convention.

Contact: baggageservices@meridianair.com | +1-800-MERIDIAN option 5.
