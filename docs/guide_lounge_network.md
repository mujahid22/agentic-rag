# Meridian Airlines — Lounge Network Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-AP-001
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All passengers entitled to Meridian lounge access

---

## 1. Lounge Tiers

Meridian Airlines operates two lounge brands at its hub airports:

| Lounge Brand | Target Passenger | Location |
|---|---|---|
| Meridian Horizon Lounge | Business class + Gold/Platinum members | DXB, LHR, JFK + 12 spoke airports |
| Meridian First Lounge | First class + Platinum members (+ Gold in F cabin) | DXB, LHR only |

---

## 2. Access Entitlements

| Passenger Type | Meridian Horizon Lounge | Meridian First Lounge |
|---|---|---|
| First class | Yes (+ 2 guests) | Yes (+ 1 guest) |
| Business class | Yes (+ 1 guest) | No (purchasable day pass: USD 120) |
| Meridian Miles Platinum | Yes (+ 2 guests) | Yes when travelling Business or First |
| Meridian Miles Gold | Yes (self + 1 guest) | No |
| Meridian Miles Silver | No (purchasable day pass: USD 45) | No |
| Economy Flex (no status) | No (purchasable day pass: USD 45) | No |
| Economy Saver | No (purchasable day pass: USD 45) | No |

---

## 4. Meridian Horizon Lounge Facilities

All Meridian Horizon Lounges include:
- Hot and cold buffet service (updated every 2 hours)
- Full bar service (beer, wine, spirits, non-alcoholic)
- High-speed Wi-Fi (password on entry card)
- Individual workstations and power sockets
- Shower suites (6 cabins at hub airports; booking via lounge receptionist)
- Business centre (printing, scanning, fax)
- Quiet zones and relaxation area
- TV screens with news and sports

---

## 5. Meridian First Lounge Facilities (DXB and LHR)

In addition to all Horizon Lounge facilities:
- À la carte restaurant (full menu service)
- Premium bar with champagne by the glass
- Private dining rooms (bookable for up to 6 guests; contact lounge host)
- Spa suite: massages, facials, manicures (additional fee; book in advance)
- Dedicated suite-style seating pods
- Personal lounge host service
- Fast-track through immigration available at DXB

Spa bookings: firstlounge.spa@meridianair.com or at lounge reception. 48-hour advance booking recommended.

---

## 6. Partner Lounges

At airports without a Meridian-owned lounge, Meridian Airlines passengers with lounge entitlement may access **partner lounges**. Partner lounge access is available in the same booking class (Business equivalent or higher) at over 600 airports worldwide via Priority Pass or equivalent programme.

Business class passengers and Meridian Gold/Platinum members on Meridian flights receive a single-use partner lounge pass at the check-in counter when no Meridian lounge is available.

---

## 7. Lounge Etiquette

- Dress code: smart casual. Sportswear, beachwear, and overly casual attire not permitted.
- Mobile phone calls: permitted in designated areas only
- Minimum age: children under 2 admitted free; children 2–12 must be accompanied by an adult
- Alcohol service: Meridian reserves the right to decline service to intoxicated guests
