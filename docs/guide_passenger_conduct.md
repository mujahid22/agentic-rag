# Meridian Airlines — Passenger Conduct Policy
**Policy Reference:** MA-HS-003
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All passengers on all Meridian Airlines flights and at Meridian-operated facilities

---

## 1. Expected Standards of Conduct

All passengers travelling on Meridian Airlines are expected to:
- Treat crew and fellow passengers with respect
- Comply promptly with all crew instructions
- Follow safety regulations at all times
- Behave in a manner that does not compromise the safety, security, or comfort of other passengers

---

## 2. Disruptive Behaviour — Definition

Disruptive behaviour includes, but is not limited to:
- Verbal abuse, intimidation, or threats directed at crew or passengers
- Physical assault or threatening physical contact
- Refusal to comply with safety instructions (seatbelt, device usage, smoking prohibition)
- Excessive alcohol consumption causing nuisance
- Sexual harassment or offensive behaviour
- Deliberate damage to aircraft or Meridian property
- Interfering with crew duties
- Attempting to access restricted areas of the aircraft (flight deck, crew rest areas)

---

## 3. Meridian Crew Authority

Meridian cabin crew are empowered under international aviation law (Tokyo Convention, Chicago Convention) to take all reasonable measures to restrain or remove a disruptive passenger. Crew authority to issue lawful instructions supersedes passenger preferences. Non-compliance is a criminal offence in most jurisdictions.

The **Purser / Cabin Manager** is the senior crew authority in the cabin. Escalation goes to the **Captain**, who has final authority over all matters onboard.

---

## 4. Alcohol Policy

### 4.1 Service of Alcohol
- Meridian cabin crew follow a responsible service of alcohol policy
- Crew may decline to serve further alcohol to any passenger who appears intoxicated
- Alcohol is not served to passengers who appear to be under 18 years of age (proof of age may be requested)
- Crew may not be pressured to serve alcohol against their judgment

### 4.2 Passenger-Brought Alcohol
Passengers may not consume alcohol brought onboard. Only alcohol served by Meridian cabin crew is permitted.

### 4.3 Refusal to Board
Meridian ground staff may deny boarding to passengers who appear visibly intoxicated and whose behaviour poses a risk to safety. No refund is issued in such cases; standard no-show policy applies.

---

## 5. Consequences of Disruptive Behaviour

| Severity | Immediate Action | Follow-up |
|---|---|---|
| Minor (verbal rudeness, noise complaint) | Verbal warning from crew; moved to different seat if available | Incident report filed |
| Moderate (persistent non-compliance, verbal aggression) | Formal warning; alcohol service stopped; continued monitoring | Possible ban review |
| Serious (physical threats, assault, safety interference) | Restraint if necessary; diversion to nearest airport; police handoff | Prosecution + permanent ban |

### 5.1 Meridian No-Fly Ban
Passengers found to have committed serious disruptive behaviour may be placed on the Meridian No-Fly List:
- Short-term ban: 12 months for moderate incidents
- Permanent ban: for assault, safety interference, or repeat incidents
- Ban reference format: **MA-NFB-XXXXXX** (e.g., MA-NFB-XKP192)

No-fly ban decisions are made by the Meridian Safety and Security team, not individual crew. Appeals may be submitted to security@meridianair.com within **30 days**.

---

## 6. Incident Reporting

Crew members file an **Air Safety Report (ASR)** for all disruptive passenger incidents. Serious incidents are also reported to:
- The aviation authority of the country of aircraft registration
- The destination country's aviation authority (if diversion occurs)
- Local law enforcement (where applicable)

Passengers who witness disruptive behaviour should report it discreetly to cabin crew using the call button or by writing a note if verbal reporting feels unsafe.

---

## 7. Ground Facility Conduct

The same standards of conduct apply at:
- Meridian check-in and boarding areas
- Meridian lounges (Horizon and First)
- Meridian Airport Ticket Offices

Meridian security staff at these facilities have authority to refuse service or entry to any individual behaving in a manner that breaches this policy.
