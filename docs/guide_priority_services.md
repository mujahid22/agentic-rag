# Meridian Airlines — Priority Services Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-AN-004
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** Economy passengers purchasing priority add-ons

---

## 1. Priority Check-in

### 1.1 What It Includes
Access to the dedicated Meridian Priority check-in lane at the airport. Available at all Meridian hub and major spoke airports.

### 1.2 Who Gets It By Default
- Business and First class passengers (included)
- Meridian Miles Gold and Platinum members (included)
- Meridian Silver members (dedicated Silver lane, separate from Priority)

### 1.3 Priority Check-in Add-On for Economy

| Route Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Domestic | USD 8 |
| International | USD 12 |

Purchase via: meridianair.com → Manage Booking → PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) → Priority Services.

---

## 2. Priority Security Fast Track

Available at Meridian hub airports (DXB, LHR, JFK) where dedicated security lanes are maintained by Meridian in partnership with the airport authority.

| Route Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Domestic | USD 10 |
| International | USD 15 |

Note: Priority security is an airport-operated service. Meridian facilitates access but cannot guarantee queue times are shorter at all times.

---

## 3. Priority Boarding

### 3.1 What It Includes
Board in **Zone 4** (with Gold members and families with infants), before general economy boarding. Ensures overhead bin space is available.

### 3.2 Priority Boarding Fee

| Route Type | Pre-Purchase Fee | Airport Fee |
|---|---|---|
| Domestic | USD 10 | USD 15 |
| International Short-Haul | USD 12 | USD 18 |
| International Long-Haul | USD 15 | USD 22 |

### 3.3 Priority Boarding Reference
Upon purchase, a priority boarding indicator is added to the boarding pass (displayed as "PRIORITY" above the zone number). Reference format: **MA-PB-XXXXXX** (e.g., MA-PB-XKP192).

---

## 4. Priority Bag Drop

### 4.1 What It Includes
Use the dedicated Priority Bag Drop lane at the airport rather than the general bag drop queue.

| Route Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Domestic | USD 8 |
| International | USD 10 |

---

## 5. Priority Baggage Tag

Economy passengers who purchase Priority Baggage Tagging receive:
- A **Priority tag** (format: MA-PRI-XXXXXXXX) on all checked bags
- Bags are loaded last and offloaded first
- Reduces baggage collection wait time

| Route Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Domestic | USD 10 |
| International | USD 15 |

---

## 6. Meridian Priority Bundle

For the best value, the Meridian Priority Bundle combines all priority services:

| Bundle | Includes | Domestic | International |
|---|---|---|---|
| Priority Essentials | Priority check-in + priority boarding | USD 15 | USD 22 |
| Priority Full | Priority check-in + security fast-track + priority boarding + priority bag tag | USD 32 | USD 48 |

Bundles must be purchased at least **4 hours** before departure. All priority services are non-refundable within 24 hours of departure.
