# Meridian Airlines — Child Fare Policy
**Policy Reference:** MA-FA-002
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** Children aged 2–11 years

---

## 1. Child Age Definition

A **child** on Meridian Airlines is a passenger aged **2 to 11 years** on the date of the outbound departure. Children must hold their own seat and ticket.

- Age 2–11: Child fare applies
- Age 12 and above: Adult fare applies (young persons receive no fare discount)
- Under 2: Infant fare applies (see MA-FA-001)

---

## 2. Child Fare Discounts

Child fares are discounted from the applicable adult fare for the same routing and fare class:

| Route Type | Child Discount |
|---|---|
| Domestic | 25% off the applicable adult fare |
| International Short-Haul | 25% off the applicable adult fare |
| International Long-Haul | 33% off the applicable adult fare |

Child discounts apply to the **base fare only**. Taxes, airport fees, and surcharges are charged at the full adult rate (as mandated by most governments and airports).

Child discounts are applied automatically when the passenger's date of birth is entered at booking. Proof of age (passport or birth certificate) may be requested at check-in.

---

## 3. Child Ticket — What's Included

Children's tickets include the same cabin entitlements as the adult fare class booked:
- Same checked baggage allowance as accompanying adult
- Same seat entitlements
- Same meal entitlements

### 3.1 Child Meals
A child meal (CHML) is available as a special meal request for children aged 2–12 years at no additional charge. Must be ordered at least 24 hours before departure (see MA-SS-004).

---

## 4. Travel Requirements for Children

### 4.1 Accompanied Children (Under 12)
Children under 12 must travel with an accompanying adult (aged 18 or over) on the same booking, unless enrolled in the Unaccompanied Minor (UM) service (MA-SS-002).

### 4.2 Young Persons (12–17)
Passengers aged 12–17 may travel independently as adults. Optional UM service available for ages 12–15 (see MA-SS-002).

---

## 5. Child ID Requirements

| Route Type | Acceptable ID for Children |
|---|---|
| Domestic | Birth certificate or national ID |
| International | Valid passport (most countries do not permit children on a parent's passport) |

Children's passports must also meet the 6-month validity requirement at the time of return travel (see MA-CK-004).

---

## 6. Booking Child Tickets

Child tickets can be booked through all standard channels:
- Meridian website (select passenger type "Child" and enter date of birth)
- Meridian app
- Contact Centre: +1-800-MERIDIAN

**Booking PNR covers all passengers in the same booking** (e.g., 2 adults + 1 child on PNR MA-XKPL92). Each passenger has a separate ticket number (MA-XXXXXXXXXXXXX).

---

## 7. Seat Seating Guarantee for Children

Meridian Airlines guarantees that children under 12 are seated adjacent to at least one accompanying adult **at no extra charge** when booked on the same PNR at least 48 hours before departure. If Meridian cannot seat the child adjacent to an adult due to aircraft change or full flight, Meridian will upgrade the group to available adjacent seats at no charge. See MA-CK-003 for seat selection details.
