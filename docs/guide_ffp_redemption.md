# Meridian Airlines — Meridian Miles Redemption Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-MM-002
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All Meridian Miles members (account format: MA-XXXXXXXX)

---

## 1. Award Ticket Redemption

### 1.1 Miles Required (One Way, per Person)

| Route Type | Economy Saver Award | Economy Flex Award | Business Award | First Award |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Domestic (under 1,500 km) | 8,000 miles | 12,000 miles | 25,000 miles | 40,000 miles |
| Short-Haul International | 15,000 miles | 20,000 miles | 40,000 miles | 65,000 miles |
| Long-Haul International | 30,000 miles | 40,000 miles | 70,000 miles | 110,000 miles |

Return awards are priced at **1.9× the one-way rate** (10% return discount).

### 1.2 Taxes and Fees on Award Tickets
Award tickets are not free — government taxes, airport fees, and carrier-imposed surcharges are charged in cash at time of booking. Typical tax/fee range: USD 50–350 per round trip depending on route.

---

## 2. Award Availability

Award seats are separate from commercial seats. Availability varies by route and season.

- **Economy Saver Award:** Limited availability; may not be available on peak travel dates
- **Economy Flex Award:** Better availability; open on most dates
- **Business Award:** Subject to Business cabin load factors; best availability 6+ months before travel
- **First Award:** Most restricted; recommended to book 9–12 months in advance

Award availability can be checked at meridianair.com/awardSearch or via the Meridian app → Redeem Miles.

---

## 3. How to Book an Award Ticket

1. Log in to Meridian Miles account (MA-XXXXXXXX)
2. Go to meridianair.com → Book → Award Ticket
3. Select origin, destination, dates, and cabin class
4. Confirm miles balance is sufficient
5. Enter passenger details (name must match Meridian Miles account or companion booking)
6. Pay applicable taxes and fees
7. Award booking confirmation issued with PNR format: **MA-AWD-XXXXXX** (e.g., MA-AWD-XKP192)

---

## 4. Award Ticket Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| Booking window | Up to 355 days in advance; minimum 24 hours before departure |
| Changes | Permitted; USD 50 change fee (waived for Gold/Platinum) |
| Cancellation | Miles re-credited; USD 50 reinstatement fee (waived for Gold/Platinum) |
| No-show | Miles forfeited on Economy Saver Award; refunded (less fee) on Flex/Business/First |
| Transferability | Awards are non-transferable to another member |
| Companion Award | Gold/Platinum members may book one companion on an award at 50% miles |

---

## 5. Upgrade Awards

Miles may be used to upgrade a purchased ticket. See MA-FK-003 Section 4 for upgrade miles rates.

---

## 6. Non-Flight Redemptions

| Redemption Category | Typical Rate |
|---|---|
| Hotel stays (partner hotels) | From 8,000 miles per night |
| Car rental (partner agencies) | From 5,000 miles per day |
| Meridian Shop merchandise | Varies (minimum 2,000 miles) |
| Charitable donation | 1,000 miles minimum |
| Miles to cash (Meridian Wallet) | 100 miles = USD 0.50 (minimum 5,000 miles) |

Miles-to-cash redemption rate: 100 miles = USD 0.50. This is the lowest-value redemption option; flight awards typically offer better value.

---

## 7. Family Miles Pooling

Meridian Miles members may pool miles with immediate family members:
- Maximum 8 members per pool
- Pool organiser must be Gold or Platinum tier
- Pooled miles can be used by any pool member for award bookings
- Setup: meridianair.com/familymiles or Contact Centre +1-800-MERIDIAN

---

## 8. Miles Purchase and Transfer

Members may purchase additional miles at a rate of **USD 0.035 per mile** (minimum 1,000 miles, maximum 30,000 miles per transaction per year) via meridianair.com/buymiles. Miles may be transferred to another Meridian Miles member at USD 0.02 per mile (minimum 1,000 miles).
