# Meridian Airlines — Name Correction & Change Policy
**Policy Reference:** MA-BK-003
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All fare classes, all routes

---

## 1. Overview

Meridian Airlines distinguishes between two types of name changes:

- **Minor Name Correction:** A typographical error of up to 3 characters in the passenger's first or last name.
- **Full Name Transfer:** A complete change of passenger identity on a ticket.

These are handled differently in terms of fees, documentation, and permissibility.

---

## 2. Minor Name Corrections

### 2.1 Definition
A minor correction involves fixing a typographical error of **1 to 3 characters** in the first name, last name, or both — provided the ticket clearly corresponds to the same individual.

Examples of permitted minor corrections:
- JONH → JOHN (1 character transposition)
- SMTH → SMITH (1 missing character)
- ALXANDER → ALEXANDER (1 missing character)

### 2.2 Fees

| Fare Class | Correction Fee | Time Limit |
|---|---|---|
| Economy Saver | USD 50 per person | Up to 24 hours before departure |
| Economy Flex | USD 25 per person | Up to 2 hours before departure |
| Business | USD 0 (free) | Up to 2 hours before departure |
| First | USD 0 (free) | Up to 2 hours before departure |

### 2.3 How to Request
Minor corrections must be submitted via:
- Meridian Contact Centre: +1-800-MERIDIAN
- Airport Ticket Office (if within 24 hours of departure)

Online self-service name corrections are **not** available. A copy of the passenger's travel document is required to verify the correct spelling. Always quote your PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) when calling.

---

## 3. Full Name Transfers

### 3.1 Definition
A full name transfer replaces one passenger's identity on a ticket with a different person. This is treated as a cancellation of the original ticket and a new booking.

### 3.2 Permissibility by Fare Class

| Fare Class | Full Transfer Permitted? |
|---|---|
| Economy Saver | Not permitted |
| Economy Flex | Not permitted |
| Business | Permitted (see fees below) |
| First | Permitted (see fees below) |

Full name transfers are not permitted within 72 hours of departure on any fare class.

### 3.3 Fees for Full Transfers (Business and First)

| Transfer Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Same-family transfer (immediate family member) | USD 100 per ticket |
| Non-family transfer | USD 200 per ticket |

Fare difference between original ticket and current fare applies in addition to the transfer fee.

### 3.4 Required Documentation
- Original passenger's government-issued ID
- New passenger's government-issued ID
- For same-family transfers: proof of relationship (marriage certificate, birth certificate)

---

## 4. Title and Suffix Corrections

Corrections to titles (Mr, Mrs, Dr, Prof) and suffixes (Jr, Sr, III) are permitted free of charge on all fare classes up to 4 hours before departure via the Contact Centre, provided they do not alter the substantive name.

---

## 5. Meridian Miles Name Mismatch

If the name on the ticket does not match the name registered to the Meridian Miles account (e.g., MA-88234410), miles will not be credited. It is the passenger's responsibility to ensure name consistency. Retroactive name correction on the Meridian Miles account to match a historical ticket is permitted once per calendar year at no charge by contacting meridiamiles@meridianair.com.

---

## 6. Legal Name Changes

Passengers who have legally changed their name (marriage, court order) may request a free name correction on any active ticket by presenting:
- The original ticket PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92)
- Legal name change documentation (marriage certificate, court order)
- Both the old and new government-issued IDs

Legal name change corrections are processed only through the Meridian Contact Centre and take up to 24 hours.
