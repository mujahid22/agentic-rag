# Meridian Airlines — Special Meals Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-SS-004
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All Meridian Airlines international flights

---

## 1. Special Meal Service

Meridian Airlines offers special meals for passengers with dietary requirements, religious observances, or medical needs on all international flights where a meal service is included. Special meals must be pre-ordered; they are not available as a last-minute in-flight request.

Special meals are available on:
- All international flights (Economy Flex, Business, First — meals included)
- Economy Saver on international flights over 5 hours (one meal included)
- Economy Saver on flights under 5 hours: purchase required (special meal upgrade fee USD 15)

## 3. Ordering Special Meals

### 3.1 Ordering Deadline
Special meals must be ordered at least **24 hours before departure**. Orders cannot be accepted within 24 hours.

### 3.2 How to Order
1. **Online:** meridianair.com → Manage Booking → Enter PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) → Add Special Meal
2. **Meridian App:** My Bookings → Select flight → Special Services → Special Meal
3. **Contact Centre:** +1-800-MERIDIAN (no service fee)

### 3.3 Confirmation
Special meal requests are confirmed and visible in the booking. The meal code appears on the booking confirmation and boarding pass data.

---

## 4. Important Notes

- **Allergy disclaimer:** Meridian Airlines kitchens handle common allergens including nuts, gluten, dairy, and shellfish. We cannot guarantee a 100% allergen-free environment. Passengers with severe allergies should carry their own food and emergency medication (e.g., EpiPen).
- **Meal availability:** Special meals are loaded per booking. If the wrong meal is loaded due to a catering error, cabin crew will attempt to accommodate from available stock but cannot guarantee an exact replacement.
- **Nut allergies:** Meridian Airlines does not serve nut-based snacks on flights where a nut allergy has been declared on the booking. However, other passengers may bring nuts onboard.
- **Business and First class:** Meals are prepared à la carte by chefs and special dietary needs are accommodated more flexibly. Contact premium.dining@meridianair.com for customised requests.

## 5. Infant and Child Meals

| Meal Type | Age Range | Code | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Baby meal | 0–24 months | BBML | Purée/stage foods; order via Contact Centre |
| Child meal | 2–12 years | CHML | Kid-friendly portions, balanced nutrition |

Baby meals must be requested at least **48 hours** in advance through the Contact Centre. Breast milk and formula are accepted in unlimited quantities in carry-on (see MA-BG-001).
