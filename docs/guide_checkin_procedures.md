# Meridian Airlines — Check-in Procedures Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-CK-001
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All fare classes, all routes

---

## 1. Check-in Channels

### 1.1 Online Check-in
Available at meridianair.com or the Meridian app.

| Route Type | Opens | Closes |
|---|---|---|
| Domestic | 24 hours before departure | 45 minutes before departure |
| International Short-Haul | 48 hours before departure | 60 minutes before departure |
| International Long-Haul | 48 hours before departure | 90 minutes before departure |

Online check-in allows seat selection, boarding pass download, and baggage declaration. Passengers with checked baggage must still drop bags at the airport (see Section 3).

### 1.2 Mobile Check-in
The Meridian app supports mobile boarding passes for domestic and international travel. Download the boarding pass to your phone's wallet (Apple Wallet, Google Wallet).

### 1.3 Airport Kiosk Check-in
Self-service kiosks are available at all Meridian-served airports. Kiosk check-in opens 3 hours before domestic departures and 4 hours before international departures.

### 1.4 Airport Counter Check-in

| Route Type | Counter Opens | Counter Closes |
|---|---|---|
| Domestic | 2.5 hours before departure | 45 minutes before departure |
| International Short-Haul | 3 hours before departure | 60 minutes before departure |
| International Long-Haul | 4 hours before departure | 75 minutes before departure |

Dedicated counters for Business and First class passengers, and Meridian Miles Gold/Platinum members.

---

## 2. Check-in Requirements

All passengers must present at check-in:
- Valid booking confirmation (PNR e.g., MA-XKPL92 or e-ticket MA-XXXXXXXXXXXXX)
- Government-issued photo ID (passport for international travel, national ID or passport for domestic)
- Visa documentation (where applicable — see MA-VI-001)

---

## 3. Baggage Drop

Passengers who checked in online or via the app but have checked baggage must use the **Bag Drop** counter:
- Opens and closes at the same times as the airport counter (Section 1.4)
- Business and First class passengers: dedicated bag drop lane
- Meridian Miles Gold/Platinum: priority bag drop lane

---

## 4. Priority Check-in

The following passengers are entitled to use priority check-in lanes:
- First class passengers
- Business class passengers
- Meridian Miles Gold and Platinum members
- Passengers travelling with infants (under 2 years)
- Passengers requiring special assistance

Priority check-in passengers also receive priority baggage tags (tag format: **MA-PRI-XXXXXXXX**), ensuring their bags are loaded last and offloaded first.

---

## 5. Late Check-in and Missed Check-in

If a passenger misses the check-in deadline:
- **Economy Saver:** No rebooking at original fare; subject to no-show policy (MA-CX-004)
- **Economy Flex:** USD 50 rebooking fee to the next available flight
- **Business:** Rebooking to next available flight at no charge
- **First:** Rebooking to next available flight at no charge

Meridian Airlines does not hold flights for late-arriving passengers.

---

## 6. Automated Passport Control (APC) and Biometric Check-in

At Meridian hub airports (DXB, LHR, JFK), biometric facial recognition check-in is available for enrolled passengers. Enrolment via the Meridian app under Profile → Biometric ID. Biometric check-in bypasses the document verification queue.
