# Meridian Airlines — General Booking Policy
**Policy Reference:** MA-BK-001
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All fare classes, all routes, all passengers

---

## 1. Booking Channels

Meridian Airlines tickets may be purchased through the following authorised channels:

- **Meridian Website:** www.meridianair.com (24/7 availability)
- **Meridian Mobile App:** iOS and Android (24/7 availability)
- **Meridian Contact Centre:** Available 24/7 at +1-800-MERIDIAN (+1-800-637-4326)
- **Authorised Travel Agents:** IATA-accredited agents with valid ARC/BSP participation
- **Airport Ticket Offices:** Available during operating hours at all Meridian-served airports

Bookings made through unauthorised third-party resellers are not guaranteed and Meridian Airlines accepts no liability for such transactions.

---

## 2. Booking Window

| Route Type | Minimum Advance | Maximum Advance |
|---|---|---|
| Domestic | 2 hours before departure | 355 days |
| International Short-Haul | 3 hours before departure | 355 days |
| International Long-Haul | 4 hours before departure | 355 days |

Bookings made within 24 hours of departure must be completed via the Contact Centre or Airport Ticket Office.

---

## 3. Payment Rules

### 3.1 Accepted Payment Methods
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- Meridian Wallet (prepaid balance)
- Meridian Miles points (full or partial redemption)
- Bank transfer (corporate accounts only; minimum 72 hours processing)
- Cash (Airport Ticket Offices only)

### 3.2 Payment Deadlines
- **Economy Saver:** Full payment required at time of booking. Seat is not held without payment.
- **Economy Flex:** Full payment required within 24 hours of booking initiation.
- **Business:** Full payment required within 48 hours of booking initiation.
- **First:** Full payment required within 48 hours of booking initiation.
- **Group Bookings (10+ passengers):** 20% deposit at booking; balance due 30 days before departure.

### 3.3 Currency
All fares are displayed and charged in USD unless the booking originates in a country where local currency pricing is mandated by regulation. Currency conversion fees may apply for non-USD cards.

---

## 4. Booking Confirmation

Upon successful payment, a booking confirmation is issued containing:

- **PNR (Passenger Name Record):** A unique 6-character alphanumeric code (e.g., MA-XKPL92) that serves as the booking reference across all Meridian Airlines systems.
- **e-Ticket Number:** A 13-digit numeric code prefixed with MA (e.g., MA-1762345678901) representing the electronic ticket.
- **Itinerary Details:** Flight number, origin, destination, date, time, fare class, seat assignment (if selected).

Confirmation is sent via email within 5 minutes of payment. If not received within 30 minutes, passengers should contact the Contact Centre with their payment reference.

---

## 5. Passenger Name Requirements

Passenger names must exactly match the government-issued travel document to be used for the journey.

- Names must be entered as: **LAST NAME / FIRST NAME MIDDLE NAME**
- Titles (Mr, Mrs, Dr) are optional and do not appear on the boarding pass.
- Suffixes (Jr, Sr) must be included if present on the travel document.
- Spaces, hyphens, and apostrophes in names must be entered as shown on the document.

**Meridian Airlines is not liable for denied boarding due to name discrepancies between the ticket and travel document.**

---

## 6. Itinerary Rules

### 6.1 One-Way and Return Tickets
Both one-way and return tickets are available on all routes. Return tickets on Economy Saver fare may carry restrictions on minimum stay requirements — refer to the fare rules at time of booking.

### 6.2 Open-Jaw Tickets
Open-jaw itineraries (fly into one city, return from another) are permitted on Economy Flex, Business, and First class tickets. Not available on Economy Saver.

### 6.3 Stopover
A stopover of more than 24 hours at an intermediate point is permitted on Business and First class tickets only. Economy fares do not permit stopovers.

---

## 7. Booking Hold (Option)

Meridian Airlines offers a **Fare Hold** option on selected routes:

| Fare Class | Hold Period | Hold Fee |
|---|---|---|
| Economy Flex | 24 hours | USD 15 |
| Business | 48 hours | USD 30 |
| First | 72 hours | USD 50 |

The hold fee is non-refundable. If payment is not completed within the hold period, the booking is automatically cancelled and the fare is released. Economy Saver fares cannot be held.

---

## 8. Multi-City Bookings

Multi-city itineraries (more than two separate flight segments) are available via the Meridian Airlines website under the "Multi-City" booking tab. Each segment is priced independently based on its applicable fare class. Multi-city bookings involving three or more carriers require contact with the Meridian Contact Centre at +1-800-MERIDIAN.
