# Meridian Airlines — Excess Baggage Fees
**Policy Reference:** MA-BG-003
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All fare classes, all routes

---

## 1. Pre-Purchase vs Airport Rates

| Purchase Channel | Discount vs Airport Rate |
|---|---|
| Meridian website/app (24+ hours before) | 30% discount |
| Meridian website/app (within 24 hours) | 20% discount |
| Airport check-in counter | Full rate (no discount) |
| Airport self-service kiosk | 10% discount |

Pre-purchase via: meridianair.com → Manage Booking → Enter PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) → Add Baggage.

## 3. Overweight Fees (Pre-Purchase Rates)

| Weight Band | Domestic | Intl Short-Haul | Intl Long-Haul |
|---|---|---|---|
| 24–32 kg | USD 40 | USD 60 | USD 80 |
| 33–45 kg | USD 80 | USD 120 | USD 160 |

---

## 4. Economy Saver First Checked Bag (Domestic Only)

Economy Saver on domestic routes receives no free checked bag. First bag fees:

| Channel | Fee |
|---|---|
| Pre-purchase 24+ hours | USD 25 |
| Pre-purchase within 24 hours | USD 30 |
| Airport counter | USD 45 |

Economy Saver on international routes includes 1 free checked bag (23 kg) as per MA-BG-002.

---

## 5. Meridian Baggage Bundles

| Bundle | Includes | Domestic | International |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter Bundle | 1 checked bag (23 kg) + priority boarding | USD 45 | USD 65 |
| Plus Bundle | 2 checked bags (23 kg each) + priority boarding + 1 kg extra carry-on | USD 75 | USD 110 |

Bundles must be purchased at least 4 hours before departure. Pre-purchased baggage is non-refundable if unused, unless Meridian Airlines cancels the flight.

---

## 6. Sporting Equipment

Sporting equipment is subject to standard oversize/excess bag fees. See MA-BG-004 for item-specific rates. Sporting items use the oversize fee schedule, not the additional bag schedule.
