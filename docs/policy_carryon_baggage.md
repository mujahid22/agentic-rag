# Meridian Airlines — Carry-On Baggage Policy
**Policy Reference:** MA-BG-001
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All fare classes, all routes

---

## 1. Carry-On Allowance by Fare Class

| Fare Class | Cabin Bag | Personal Item | Total |
|---|---|---|---|
| Economy Saver | 1 bag, max 7 kg | 1 item, max 2 kg | 9 kg |
| Economy Flex | 1 bag, max 10 kg | 1 item, max 3 kg | 13 kg |
| Business | 2 bags, max 14 kg total | 1 item, max 3 kg | 17 kg |
| First | 2 bags, max 16 kg total | 1 item, max 3 kg | 19 kg |

---

## 2. Cabin Bag Size Limits

The cabin bag must fit in the overhead bin. Maximum dimensions:

| Measurement | Limit |
|---|---|
| Length | 56 cm (22 inches) |
| Width | 36 cm (14 inches) |
| Depth | 23 cm (9 inches) |
| Combined (L+W+D) | 115 cm (45 inches) |

These dimensions include wheels, handles, and exterior pockets. Bags exceeding these dimensions will be gate-checked at the passenger's expense (USD 35).

---

## 3. Personal Item Dimensions

A personal item must fit under the seat in front. Maximum dimensions:

| Measurement | Limit |
|---|---|
| Length | 40 cm (16 inches) |
| Width | 30 cm (12 inches) |
| Depth | 15 cm (6 inches) |

Accepted personal items: handbag, laptop bag, small backpack, camera bag, duty-free shopping bag (single, soft-sided).

---

## 4. Prohibited Items in Cabin Baggage

- Sharp objects: knives, scissors with blades over 6 cm, box cutters
- Liquids over 100 ml per container
- Aerosols over 100 ml
- Lighters — one disposable lighter permitted in pocket only
- Firearms, replicas, or imitation weapons
- Lithium batteries over 160 Wh
- Spare/loose lithium batteries over 100 Wh must be in carry-on (not checked)

---

## 5. Liquids Rule (3-1-1)

- Each container: maximum **100 ml**
- All containers in: **1 clear resealable plastic bag, max 1 litre**
- **1 bag** per passenger

Exceptions (must be declared at security — no quantity limit):
- Baby formula and breast milk for infants on the Meridian booking
- Essential medications with prescription documentation
- Duty-free liquids purchased post-security in sealed tamper-evident bags

---

## 6. Electronic Devices

- Laptops must be removed from bags and placed in a separate security tray
- Power banks permitted in carry-on only (not checked). Maximum: 100 Wh per unit, 2 units maximum.

---

## 7. Gate-Check Policy

If overhead bin space is unavailable, cabin bags may be gate-checked at no charge and returned at the jetway on arrival. Passengers retain their personal item onboard. Meridian Airlines is not responsible for damage to fragile items in gate-checked bags.

Passengers who voluntarily gate-check an oversized cabin bag are charged USD 35.

---

## 8. Infant Carry-On

Infants on a lap ticket are permitted one small bag (max 5 kg) for infant essentials at no additional charge, in addition to the travelling adult's allowance. This bag must fit under the seat.
