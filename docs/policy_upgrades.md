# Meridian Airlines — Upgrade Policy
**Policy Reference:** MA-FK-003
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All fare classes, all routes

---

## 1. Types of Upgrades

Meridian Airlines offers three upgrade mechanisms:

| Type | How | Who |
|---|---|---|
| Paid Upgrade | Pay fare difference in cash | All passengers |
| Bid Upgrade | Submit a bid; Meridian accepts if seat available | All passengers |
| Miles Upgrade | Redeem Meridian Miles | Meridian Miles members (all tiers) |

---

## 2. Paid Upgrades

### 2.1 Eligibility
Paid upgrades are available on all fare classes subject to seat availability in the higher cabin.

### 2.2 Upgrade Path and Fees

| From | To | Typical Fee Range (per person per sector) |
|---|---|---|
| Economy Saver | Economy Flex | USD 75–150 |
| Economy Saver | Business | USD 400–1,200 (route-dependent) |
| Economy Saver | First | USD 800–2,500 (route-dependent) |
| Economy Flex | Business | USD 300–1,000 (route-dependent) |
| Economy Flex | First | USD 700–2,200 (route-dependent) |
| Business | First | USD 300–800 (route-dependent) |

Exact upgrade prices are shown in real time via Manage Booking → Upgrade. Fees reflect the fare difference between the original ticket and the current lowest available fare in the higher cabin.

### 2.3 How to Purchase
- **Online:** meridianair.com → Manage Booking → Enter PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) → Upgrade
- **Contact Centre:** +1-800-MERIDIAN (USD 25 service fee applies)
- **Airport:** At check-in or upgrade desk (subject to availability)

---

## 3. Bid Upgrades (Meridian Elevate)

### 3.1 How It Works
Meridian Elevate allows Economy passengers to submit a monetary bid for an upgrade. Bids are evaluated 48 hours before departure and accepted on a highest-bid basis.

### 3.2 Bid Process
1. Receive Meridian Elevate invitation email (sent 7 days before departure to eligible bookings)
2. Submit bid amount within the minimum/maximum range shown
3. Receive decision via email 48–24 hours before departure
4. If accepted: credit card is charged automatically; boarding pass is reissued

### 3.3 Bid Ranges (approximate, route-dependent)

| Route Type | Economy → Business Bid Range |
|---|---|
| Domestic | USD 50 – USD 250 |
| International Short-Haul | USD 100 – USD 500 |
| International Long-Haul | USD 200 – USD 1,000 |

Declined bids are not charged. Only one bid per booking per sector.

---

## 4. Miles Upgrades

### 4.1 Eligibility
Meridian Miles members (all tiers) may redeem miles for upgrades subject to upgrade seat availability (different from standard seat availability).

### 4.2 Miles Required (per person, one way)

| From | To | Domestic | Intl Short-Haul | Intl Long-Haul |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Economy (any) | Business | 15,000 miles | 25,000 miles | 45,000 miles |
| Economy (any) | First | 25,000 miles | 40,000 miles | 70,000 miles |
| Business | First | 15,000 miles | 20,000 miles | 35,000 miles |

### 4.3 Co-payment
Miles upgrades require a co-payment of government taxes and airport fees for the higher cabin. The co-payment is charged to the card on file.

### 4.4 How to Request
- **Online:** meridianair.com → My Miles → Upgrade with Miles → Enter PNR
- **Contact Centre:** +1-800-MERIDIAN (no service fee for miles upgrades)
- Miles upgrades may be requested up to **2 hours before departure** subject to availability.

---

## 5. Upgrade Waitlist

If the desired upgrade cabin is full, passengers may join the upgrade waitlist:
- Waitlist priority order: Platinum → Gold → Silver → Blue (base tier)
- Within the same tier: higher miles balance takes priority
- Waitlist confirmation sent via email/SMS when upgrade clears
- Waitlist reference format: **MA-UWL-XXXXXX** (e.g., MA-UWL-XKP192)
