# Meridian Airlines — Fare Classes Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-FK-001
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All routes, all passengers

---

## 1. Overview of Meridian Fare Classes

Meridian Airlines offers four primary fare classes across all routes. Each fare class is associated with a specific cabin and a set of inclusions, restrictions, and flexibility rules.

| Fare Class | Cabin | Booking Code | Flexibility | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Economy Saver | Economy | S | Low | Budget travellers, fixed plans |
| Economy Flex | Economy | Y | Medium | Travellers needing some flexibility |
| Business | Business | C | High | Corporate and premium travellers |
| First | First | F | Maximum | Luxury travel, full flexibility |

---

## 2. Economy Saver (Code: S)

### Inclusions
- 1 personal item (up to 2 kg)
- 1 cabin bag (up to 7 kg)
- 1 checked bag on international routes (23 kg); no free checked bag on domestic
- Standard seat selection (middle seats free; window/aisle: USD 15–35)
- Basic in-flight entertainment (streaming on personal device)
- Complimentary water and non-alcoholic beverages on flights over 2 hours

### Restrictions
- Non-refundable (travel credit only upon cancellation)
- Date/time changes: USD 75–200 fee + fare difference
- Route changes: USD 150 + fare difference
- Not changeable within 48 hours of departure
- No lounge access
- Meridian Miles earning: 25% of base miles flown

### Surcharges
Economy Saver fares on international routes include a fuel surcharge of USD 15–45 depending on route length.

---

## 3. Economy Flex (Code: Y)

### Inclusions
- 1 personal item (up to 3 kg)
- 1 cabin bag (up to 10 kg)
- 1 checked bag on all routes (23 kg); 2 checked bags on long-haul international
- Standard + preferred seat selection included
- Full in-flight entertainment
- Complimentary meals on flights over 3 hours
- Complimentary alcoholic beverages on international flights

### Restrictions
- Partially refundable (cash refund after cancellation fee)
- Free date/time changes up to 2 days before departure
- Route changes: USD 75 + fare difference
- No lounge access (purchasable as add-on)
- Meridian Miles earning: 100% of base miles flown

---

## 4. Business Class (Code: C)

### Inclusions
- 1 personal item (up to 3 kg)
- 2 cabin bags (up to 14 kg total)
- 2 checked bags (up to 32 kg each) on all routes
- Any seat selection included (including lie-flat seat on long-haul)
- Dedicated Business cabin with lie-flat seats on wide-body aircraft
- Full à la carte dining, premium wines and spirits
- Access to Meridian Horizon Lounge at all hub airports (DXB, LHR, JFK)
- Priority check-in, priority security, priority boarding
- Arrival lounge access at DXB, LHR, JFK
- Meridian Miles earning: 150% of base miles flown

### Flexibility
- Free date/time and route changes at any time before departure
- Full cash refund with no cancellation fee (except within 24 hours: USD 75)
- Gold/Platinum members: zero fees at all times

---

## 5. First Class (Code: F)

### Inclusions
- 1 personal item (up to 3 kg)
- 2 cabin bags (up to 16 kg total)
- 3 checked bags (up to 32 kg each) on all routes
- Private suite seating on all long-haul Meridian aircraft
- Personalised pre-flight dining at Meridian First Lounge
- Chauffeur service to/from airport (DXB, LHR, JFK — within 50 km radius)
- Dedicated First Class check-in desk
- Access to Meridian First Lounge (separate from Business lounge)
- Spa facilities available at DXB and LHR First Lounges
- Meridian Miles earning: 200% of base miles flown

### Flexibility
- Unconditionally refundable — no fees at any time, including no-shows
- Free changes with no limits, including same-day departure changes
