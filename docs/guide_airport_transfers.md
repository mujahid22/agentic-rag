# Meridian Airlines — Airport Transfer Services Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-AP-002
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All passengers at Meridian hub airports (DXB, LHR, JFK)

---

## 1. First Class Chauffeur Service

First class passengers are entitled to a complimentary chauffeur service to/from Meridian hub airports.

### 1.1 Coverage
- Available at: Dubai (DXB), London (LHR), New York (JFK)
- Radius: within **75 km** of the airport
- Vehicle: executive sedan or SUV
- Available for arrival and departure

### 1.2 How to Book
- **Online:** meridianair.com → Manage Booking → Enter PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) → Ground Transport
- **Contact Centre:** +1-800-MERIDIAN (at least **48 hours** before travel)
- **Email:** chauffeur@meridianair.com

Chauffeur Reference format: **MA-CH-XXXXXX** (e.g., MA-CH-XKP192).

### 1.3 Limitations
- Maximum 1 vehicle per booking (accommodates up to 4 passengers + luggage)
- For larger parties, a second vehicle can be arranged at USD 80 per trip
- Cancellation: free if cancelled 24+ hours before; USD 50 if cancelled within 24 hours

---

## 2. Business Class Airport Bus Service

Business class passengers at DXB and LHR have access to Meridian's dedicated **Business Express Bus** service:
- Runs every 30 minutes between the city centre and the airport
- Price: Complimentary for Business class passengers on day of travel
- Business Express Bus stop: signposted at Arrivals Level, Terminal 3 (DXB) and Terminal 4 (LHR)

---

## 3. Connecting Passenger Transfer Services

Passengers connecting between Meridian Airlines flights on the same PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) and with less than **90 minutes** connection time at a hub airport receive:
- Meridian Express Connector service: dedicated Meridian staff escort from arrival gate to departure gate
- Priority security and immigration processing (subject to host country immigration authority approval)
- Automatically triggered when connection is on a single PNR with under 90-minute connection time

For connections with more than 90 minutes, self-connect following standard airport signage.

---

## 4. Minimum Connection Times (MCT)

| Hub Airport | MCT (Domestic–Domestic) | MCT (International–International) | MCT (International–Domestic) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Dubai (DXB) | 45 minutes | 60 minutes | 75 minutes |
| London (LHR) | 60 minutes | 75 minutes | 90 minutes |
| New York (JFK) | 60 minutes | 90 minutes | 90 minutes |

Connections booked on a single Meridian PNR below these MCTs are flagged and may be rescheduled by Meridian. Connections made via separate bookings (self-connect) are the passenger's sole responsibility.

---

## 5. Disrupted Connection Assistance

If a Meridian flight delay causes a passenger to miss their connection on the same PNR:
- Meridian ground staff will meet passengers at the arrival gate with rebooking options
- Next available Meridian or partner airline connection arranged at no charge
- Hotel accommodation provided if no connection available within 4 hours (Business/First) or 6 hours (Economy)
- Meal vouchers provided per MA-DC-001

Contact Meridian ground staff at the Transfer Desk, located at each Meridian hub arrival area. Reference your PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) and Disruption Reference if issued (MA-DC-XXXXXX).
