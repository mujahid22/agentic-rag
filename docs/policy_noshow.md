# Meridian Airlines — No-Show Policy
**Policy Reference:** MA-CX-004
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All fare classes, all routes

---

## 1. Definition of No-Show

A passenger is classified as a **no-show** if they:
- Fail to check in before check-in closes, **or**
- Check in but fail to present at the boarding gate before gate close, **or**
- Cancel the booking after the flight has already departed

The no-show policy applies only to **passenger-initiated** failure to travel, not to airline-initiated cancellations or delays.

---

## 2. No-Show Penalties by Fare Class

| Fare Class | No-Show Penalty | Remaining Value |
|---|---|---|
| Economy Saver | Full fare forfeited | No refund, no credit |
| Economy Flex | USD 150 per person | Remainder as Meridian travel credit (MA-TC-XXXXXX, valid 12 months) |
| Business | USD 150 per person | Remainder as cash refund |
| First | USD 0 | Full cash refund |

---

## 3. Return Ticket — Effect of No-Show on Outbound

If a passenger is a no-show on the outbound leg of a return ticket, Meridian Airlines **automatically cancels the return leg** unless the passenger contacts the Contact Centre before the return departure.

| Action | Deadline | Contact |
|---|---|---|
| Notify Meridian of intent to travel on return | Before return flight check-in opens | +1-800-MERIDIAN or meridianair.com |
| Reinstate return leg | Subject to availability + reinstatement fee | Contact Centre only |

**Reinstatement Fees (Return Leg After No-Show on Outbound):**

| Fare Class | Reinstatement Fee |
|---|---|
| Economy Saver | USD 100 + fare difference |
| Economy Flex | USD 75 + fare difference |
| Business | USD 50 + fare difference |
| First | USD 0 |

---

## 4. Missed Connections Due to Passenger Fault

If a passenger misses a connecting Meridian flight due to their own fault (not an airline delay):
- The missed segment and all subsequent segments are treated as no-shows
- Economy Saver: no refund, no rebooking at original fare
- Economy Flex: USD 75 rebooking fee + fare difference for next available flight
- Business / First: rebooking at no charge on next available flight in same cabin

---

## 5. Exceptional Circumstance Waivers

Meridian Airlines may waive the no-show penalty in the following documented cases:
- Medical emergency preventing travel (hospital admission letter required)
- Death of an immediate family member (death certificate + relationship proof)
- Government-mandated travel restriction (official documentation required)

Waiver requests must be submitted within **14 days** of the missed flight to noshow.waiver@meridianair.com. Reference your PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) in all correspondence. Waivers are assessed individually and Meridian Airlines' decision is final.

---

## 6. Meridian Miles — No-Show Impact

- No Meridian Miles are earned on no-show tickets
- Elite status members (Gold/Platinum) receive no special exemption from no-show penalties but may apply for exceptional waivers under Section 5
