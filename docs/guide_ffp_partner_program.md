# Meridian Airlines — Meridian Miles Partner Programme Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-MM-004
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All Meridian Miles members (account format: MA-XXXXXXXX)

---

## 1. Programme Overview

Meridian Miles members can earn and redeem miles with a wide network of hotel, car rental, retail, and financial partners in addition to flights. Partner activity miles are credited to the same Meridian Miles account (MA-XXXXXXXX) as flight miles.

---

## 2. Hotel Partners

| Partner Hotel Chain | Earn Rate | Redeem Rate | Minimum Spend |
|---|---|---|---|
| Meridian Grand Hotels | 5 miles per USD | 8,000 miles/night | No minimum |
| Skyline Suites | 3 miles per USD | 10,000 miles/night | USD 50/night |
| Azure Resorts | 2 miles per USD | 12,000 miles/night | USD 75/night |
| Urban Stay Hotels | 2 miles per USD | 8,500 miles/night | No minimum |

To earn hotel miles: provide Meridian Miles number (MA-XXXXXXXX) at check-in or link account via the hotel partner portal. Retroactive hotel mile claims must be submitted within **3 months** of stay.

---

## 3. Car Rental Partners

| Partner | Earn Rate | Redeem Rate |
|---|---|---|
| GlobalDrive Car Rental | 3 miles per USD | 5,000 miles/day |
| CityWheels | 2 miles per USD | 6,000 miles/day |
| AutoPrime | 2 miles per USD | 5,500 miles/day |

Car rental miles require the Meridian Miles number to be added to the rental reservation. Miles are credited within **7 days** of rental completion.

---

## 4. Financial Partners (Credit Cards)

| Card Product | Earn Rate | Sign-Up Bonus |
|---|---|---|
| Meridian Miles Visa (standard) | 1 mile per USD on all spend; 2 miles on Meridian purchases | 10,000 bonus miles |
| Meridian Miles Visa (premium) | 2 miles per USD; 3 miles on Meridian; 1 mile on travel | 30,000 bonus miles |
| Meridian Miles Amex | 1.5 miles per USD; 3 miles on Meridian | 25,000 bonus miles |

Card applications: meridianair.com/creditcard. Annual fees vary by card tier. Bonus miles are credited after the first purchase and within 90 days of card approval.

---

## 5. Retail and Lifestyle Partners

| Category | Partners | Earn Rate |
|---|---|---|
| Online shopping | Meridian Mall portal | 2–10 miles per USD (varies by retailer) |
| Dining | Meridian Dining partners (1,200+ restaurants) | 3 miles per USD |
| Telecommunications | MeridianConnect mobile plans | 1 mile per USD on monthly bill |
| Insurance | Meridian Travel Insurance | 500 miles per policy purchased |

---

## 6. Miles Transfers from Other Programmes

Meridian Miles allows inbound transfers from select loyalty programmes:

| Programme | Transfer Ratio | Minimum Transfer | Processing Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| Meridian Grand Hotels Rewards | 3 hotel points : 1 Meridian mile | 3,000 hotel points | 3–5 business days |
| GlobalDrive Elite Points | 5 points : 1 Meridian mile | 5,000 points | 5–7 business days |

Outbound transfers (Meridian Miles to partner programmes) are not currently available.

---

## 7. Partner Miles Disputes

If partner miles are not credited within the stated timeframe:
1. Check eligible activity: ensure Meridian Miles number (MA-XXXXXXXX) was provided
2. Submit a missing partner miles claim at: meridianair.com/missingmiles
3. Attach proof of qualifying activity (receipt, booking confirmation)
4. Claims window: **6 months** from activity date
5. Resolution time: 15–30 business days

Contact: partnerrewards@meridianair.com for complex disputes.
