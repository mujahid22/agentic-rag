# Meridian Airlines — Booking Modifications Policy
**Policy Reference:** MA-BK-002
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All fare classes, all routes

---

## 1. What Can Be Modified

The following elements of a confirmed Meridian Airlines booking may be modified subject to fare rules and availability:

- **Travel date** (same route, different date)
- **Travel time** (same route and date, different flight)
- **Origin or destination** (route change — see Section 4)
- **Cabin class** (upgrade or downgrade)
- **Connecting point** (for multi-segment itineraries)

Passenger name changes are handled under a separate policy (MA-BK-003).

---

## 2. Modification Fees by Fare Class

### 2.1 Date and Time Changes

| Fare Class | More than 7 Days Before | 2–7 Days Before | Within 48 Hours |
|---|---|---|---|
| Economy Saver | USD 75 per person | USD 100 per person | Not permitted |
| Economy Flex | USD 0 (free) | USD 0 (free) | USD 50 per person |
| Business | USD 0 (free) | USD 0 (free) | USD 0 (free) |
| First | USD 0 (free) | USD 0 (free) | USD 0 (free) |

Fare difference applies in all cases. If the new fare is lower, no refund is issued on Economy Saver. A travel credit is issued for Economy Flex, Business, and First.

### 2.2 Domestic Route Discount
Domestic route modifications carry a 50% reduction on fees listed in Section 2.1 for Economy Saver only.

---

## 3. How to Modify a Booking

Modifications may be made through:

1. **Meridian Website / App:** Log in → Manage Booking → Enter PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) and last name → Select "Change Flight"
2. **Meridian Contact Centre:** +1-800-MERIDIAN (service fee of USD 25 applies for agent-assisted changes)
3. **Airport Ticket Office:** Subject to staff availability and departure timing

When modifying online, the new fare at time of modification applies. If the new fare is higher, the fare difference is charged immediately. If lower, a travel credit is issued (Economy Flex and above).

---

## 4. Route Changes

A route change is defined as any modification to the origin, destination, or both.

| Fare Class | Route Change Fee | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Economy Saver | USD 150 per person + fare difference | Subject to availability |
| Economy Flex | USD 75 per person + fare difference | Subject to availability |
| Business | USD 0 + fare difference | Subject to availability |
| First | USD 0 + fare difference | Subject to availability |

Route changes on Economy Saver are not permitted within 48 hours of departure.

---

## 5. Cabin Class Changes

### 5.1 Upgrades
Passengers may upgrade to a higher cabin class at any time before check-in closes, subject to seat availability. The upgrade fee is the fare difference between the original fare and the applicable fare in the new cabin. Meridian Miles may be used to cover the fare difference.

### 5.2 Downgrades
Voluntary downgrades are permitted on Economy Flex, Business, and First. A travel credit equal to the fare difference (not a cash refund) is issued. Downgrades on Economy Saver are not permitted.

---

## 6. Modification Limits

- A maximum of **3 modifications** are permitted per booking on Economy Saver.
- Economy Flex, Business, and First have no limit on the number of modifications.
- Each modification retains the original PNR and ticket number. Example PNR: MA-XKPL92.

---

## 7. Codeshare and Partner Flights

Modifications to flights operated by codeshare or interline partners must be processed through the Meridian Contact Centre at +1-800-MERIDIAN. Online self-service modification is not available for partner-operated segments. Additional partner airline fees may apply.

---

## 8. Group Booking Modifications

For group bookings (10+ passengers), modifications must be submitted in writing to groupbookings@meridianair.com at least 14 days before departure. Individual passenger modifications within a group booking are subject to group contract terms negotiated at time of booking.
