# Meridian Airlines — Alliance Benefits Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-CS-002
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** Meridian Miles members travelling on Meridian alliance partner flights

---

## 1. Meridian's Alliance Membership

Meridian Airlines is a member of the **SkyCircle Alliance**, a global airline alliance comprising 18 member carriers. Alliance membership provides passengers with reciprocal benefits across all member airlines.

SkyCircle Alliance members relevant to Meridian passengers include:
- ArcticAir (AA)
- SunPath Airways (SP)
- Pacific Rim Airlines (PR)
- EuroConnect (EC)
- Southern Cross Air (SC)
- TransAtlantic Express (TE)
- IndoPacific Air (IP)

Full member list: skycirclealliance.com/members

---

## 2. Alliance Lounge Access

| Tier | Alliance Lounge Access |
|---|---|
| Meridian Platinum | Access to any SkyCircle Business or First lounge at any alliance member airport |
| Meridian Gold | Access to SkyCircle Economy Plus lounge (where available) at alliance airports |
| Meridian Silver | No alliance lounge access (Meridian lounges only per MA-AP-001) |
| Meridian Blue | No alliance lounge access |

Access is granted on presentation of the Meridian Miles card or digital card in the Meridian app (account MA-XXXXXXXX) and a same-day boarding pass on a SkyCircle alliance flight.

---

## 3. Alliance Miles Earning

Meridian Miles members earn miles on SkyCircle Alliance partner flights at the following rates:

| Earning Class | Rate |
|---|---|
| Alliance Business / First class | 100% of miles flown |
| Alliance Economy Flex equivalent | 75% of miles flown |
| Alliance Economy Saver equivalent | 50% of miles flown |
| Alliance lowest promotional fares | 25% of miles flown |

Exact earning rates vary by partner airline and fare class combination. Full earning table at meridianair.com/earningpartners.

Always add your Meridian Miles number (MA-XXXXXXXX) to the partner booking before travel to ensure miles are credited.

---

## 4. Through Baggage Check on Alliance Flights

When travelling on a single ticket (issued under a Meridian ticket number MA-XXXXXXXXXXXXX) that includes alliance partner segments:
- Bags are tagged through to the final destination at the first check-in point
- Baggage allowance of the **ticketing carrier's policy** applies (Meridian's if the ticket is MA-issued)
- Bags do not need to be reclaimed and rechecked at alliance partner hubs

For separate tickets (self-connecting), baggage must be rechecked independently.

---

## 5. Alliance Priority Services

Meridian Miles Gold and Platinum members receive the following on all SkyCircle Alliance carrier flights:

| Benefit | Gold | Platinum |
|---|---|---|
| Priority check-in at alliance airports | Yes | Yes |
| Priority boarding | Yes | Yes |
| Extra baggage allowance | +1 bag (varies by partner) | +2 bags (varies by partner) |
| Alliance partner lounge access | Limited | Full |

Partner carriers may have different priority benefit implementations. Meridian cannot guarantee uniform service delivery across all 18 alliance members.

---

## 6. Alliance Award Redemption

Meridian Miles may be redeemed for award flights on SkyCircle Alliance member airlines:
- Miles required are based on the alliance redemption table (typically higher than Meridian own-metal awards)
- Award availability is subject to the partner airline's award inventory
- Taxes and fees are charged in cash at time of booking
- Book via meridianair.com/awardSearch → Toggle "Alliance Partners"

Alliance award booking reference format: **MA-ALA-XXXXXX** (e.g., MA-ALA-XKP192).
