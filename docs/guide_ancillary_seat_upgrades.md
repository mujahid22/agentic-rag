# Meridian Airlines — Seat Upgrade Add-Ons Guide
**Policy Reference:** MA-AN-001
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** Economy passengers on all Meridian Airlines routes

---

## 1. Available Seat Add-Ons

Economy Saver and Economy Flex passengers can enhance their seating experience by purchasing the following add-ons. These are seat-category upgrades within the Economy cabin — not cabin class upgrades (see MA-FK-003 for cabin upgrades).

---

## 2. Preferred Seat Add-On

Preferred seats include window and aisle seats and first-10-row Economy seats.

| Route Type | Pre-Purchase Fee | Airport Fee |
|---|---|---|
| Domestic (under 2 hours) | USD 12 per sector | USD 18 per sector |
| Domestic (over 2 hours) | USD 18 per sector | USD 25 per sector |
| International Short-Haul | USD 22 per sector | USD 32 per sector |
| International Long-Haul | USD 30 per sector | USD 45 per sector |

Economy Flex passengers: preferred seats are **free** (see MA-CK-003).

---

## 3. Extra Legroom Seat Add-On

Extra legroom seats offer 91 cm (36 inch) pitch — 13 cm more than standard.

| Route Type | Pre-Purchase Fee | Airport Fee |
|---|---|---|
| Domestic | USD 25–40 per sector | USD 40–55 per sector |
| International Short-Haul | USD 35–55 per sector | USD 55–75 per sector |
| International Long-Haul | USD 50–75 per sector | USD 75–100 per sector |

Fee varies by specific row. Rows closer to the exit have higher fees. Price displayed in Manage Booking → Seat Selection.

---

## 4. Exit Row Seat Add-On

Exit row seats offer the maximum legroom (107 cm / 42 inch pitch on most aircraft).

| Route Type | Pre-Purchase Fee | Airport Fee |
|---|---|---|
| Domestic | USD 30–50 per sector | USD 50–65 per sector |
| International Short-Haul | USD 45–65 per sector | USD 65–85 per sector |
| International Long-Haul | USD 60–85 per sector | USD 85–110 per sector |

**Eligibility Restrictions for Exit Row:**
- Minimum age: 15 years
- Must be physically capable of operating the emergency exit
- Must be willing to assist in an emergency
- Passengers with mobility impairments, who are pregnant, or travelling with infants are not permitted in exit rows

---

## 5. Economy Comfort Bundle

The Economy Comfort Bundle combines multiple seat add-ons with other amenities at a discount:

| Bundle | Includes | Domestic | International Long-Haul |
|---|---|---|---|
| Comfort Lite | Preferred seat + priority boarding | USD 30 | USD 55 |
| Comfort Plus | Extra legroom seat + priority boarding + 1 extra carry-on kg | USD 55 | USD 95 |
| Comfort Max | Exit row seat + priority boarding + extra bag + lounge pass | USD 100 | USD 175 |

Bundles must be purchased at least **6 hours** before departure. Bundle add-on reference format: **MA-EC-XXXXXX** (e.g., MA-EC-XKP192).

---

## 6. How to Purchase

- **Meridian Website:** Manage Booking → Enter PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) → Seat Selection / Add-Ons
- **Meridian App:** My Bookings → Select flight → Upgrade Seat
- **Contact Centre:** +1-800-MERIDIAN (USD 10 service fee applies)
- **Airport Kiosk:** 10% discount vs counter price
- **Airport Counter:** Full price

All seat add-on fees are **non-refundable** unless Meridian Airlines changes the aircraft type and the selected seat type is no longer available, in which case a full seat fee refund is issued.
