# Meridian Airlines — Ticket Validity Policy
**Policy Reference:** MA-FK-002
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All fare classes, all routes

---

## 1. Ticket Validity Periods

All Meridian Airlines tickets (e-ticket number format: MA-XXXXXXXXXXXXX) are valid for the period stated at time of purchase. The validity period is measured from the **first date of travel** on the ticket.

| Fare Class | Ticket Validity |
|---|---|
| Economy Saver | 12 months from first travel date |
| Economy Flex | 12 months from first travel date |
| Business | 12 months from first travel date |
| First | 12 months from first travel date |

If a ticket is not used and not cancelled before expiry, it is forfeited with no refund or credit.

---

## 2. Minimum Stay Requirements

Certain Economy Saver fares on specific routes carry minimum stay requirements:

- **International routes with "MIN3" condition:** The return travel date must be at least 3 days after the outbound departure date.
- **International routes with "SATNIGHT" condition:** The return travel must include a Saturday night at the destination.

Minimum stay conditions are shown at the time of fare selection and in the booking confirmation. Economy Flex, Business, and First class tickets have no minimum stay requirements.

---

## 3. Open-Jaw Tickets

An open-jaw ticket allows the passenger to fly into one city and depart from a different city.

| Type | Description | Eligible Fare Classes |
|---|---|---|
| Single open-jaw | Fly A→B, return C→A (return from different city) | Economy Flex, Business, First |
| Double open-jaw | Fly A→B, return C→D (different cities at both ends) | Business, First only |

Open-jaw tickets are not permitted on Economy Saver fares. Each sector of an open-jaw ticket is priced independently based on the applicable one-way fare.

---

## 4. Stopovers

A stopover is defined as a deliberate break in journey exceeding 24 hours at an intermediate point.

| Fare Class | Stopover Permitted | Max Stopovers | Fee per Stopover |
|---|---|---|---|
| Economy Saver | No | — | — |
| Economy Flex | No | — | — |
| Business | Yes | 2 | USD 0 |
| First | Yes | 2 | USD 0 |

Stopovers must be declared at time of booking. Adding a stopover after ticketing requires a fare recalculation.

---

## 5. Re-routing

Re-routing is a change to the itinerary that results in a different intermediate point or a different overall routing between origin and destination.

- Economy Saver: Not permitted
- Economy Flex: Permitted with USD 75 fee + fare difference
- Business / First: Permitted with USD 0 fee + fare difference (if any)

Re-routing requests must be made before the first sector departs. Re-routing after travel has begun is not permitted.

---

## 6. Ticket Extensions

If a passenger is unable to travel due to illness or injury, the ticket validity period may be extended upon presentation of a medical certificate:

- Extension period: Up to **3 months** beyond original expiry
- One extension per ticket
- Available on all fare classes
- Request must be submitted to ticketservices@meridianair.com before the ticket expires

---

## 7. Lost or Stolen Tickets

Meridian Airlines issues electronic tickets only (e-ticket format: MA-XXXXXXXXXXXXX). Electronic tickets cannot be lost or stolen. Passengers who cannot locate their booking confirmation should contact +1-800-MERIDIAN with their name and travel date to retrieve their PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92).
