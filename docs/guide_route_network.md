| London | London Heathrow | LHR | European Hub |

## 6. Recommended Routes — Dallas (DFW) to Tokyo (NRT / HND)

Meridian Airlines does not operate a direct non-stop flight between Dallas (DFW) and Tokyo. The two recommended connecting itineraries are:

### Option A — Via Los Angeles (LAX) ✅ Recommended
This is the fastest and most popular routing for Dallas to Tokyo passengers.

| Segment | Flight | Departure | Arrival | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Dallas (DFW) → Los Angeles (LAX) | MA-310 | 08:00 DFW | 09:10 LAX | 3h 10m |
| Los Angeles (LAX) → Tokyo Narita (NRT) | MA-101 | 11:30 LAX | 15:00+1 NRT | 11h 30m |
| **Total journey time** | | | | **~16h 30m** |

- Minimum connection time at LAX: 90 minutes
- MA-310 + MA-101 operates daily
- MA-310 + MA-103 (Haneda) also available for passengers preferring HND arrival

### Option B — Via Dubai (DXB)
A longer but alternative routing, suitable for passengers combining a Dubai stopover.

| Segment | Flight | Departure | Arrival | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Dallas (DFW) → London (LHR) | MA-003 | 17:00 DFW | 08:50+1 LHR | 9h 50m |
| London (LHR) → Dubai (DXB) | MA-051 | 11:00 LHR | 21:00 DXB | 7h 00m |
| Dubai (DXB) → Tokyo Narita (NRT) | MA-201 | 02:00 DXB | 16:30 NRT | 8h 30m |
| **Total journey time** | | | | **~35h+** |

Option B is not recommended for time-sensitive travel due to the significantly longer transit time.

**Recommended booking:** Book the DFW → LAX → NRT itinerary as a single Meridian PNR to ensure bag through-check and single baggage allowance.

## 9. Destinations Not Served by Meridian

Meridian Airlines does not currently operate flights to the following. Codeshare or interline connections via partner airlines may be available.

- India (except via codeshare from DXB with partner airlines)
- China Mainland (PEK, PVG) — available via codeshare via DXB or NRT
- Africa — available via codeshare through LHR or DXB

Refer to guide_codeshare_partners for partner airline options.
