# Meridian Airlines — Refund Processing Policy
**Policy Reference:** MA-CX-003
**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025
**Applies To:** All fare classes, all routes

---

## 1. Refund Eligibility Summary

| Fare Class | Cash Refund | Travel Credit |
|---|---|---|
| Economy Saver | Not eligible | Eligible (post-fee remainder) |
| Economy Flex | Eligible (post-fee remainder) | Eligible (alternative) |
| Business | Eligible (post-fee remainder) | Eligible (alternative) |
| First | Fully eligible (no fees) | N/A |
| Promotional / Sale | Not eligible | Not eligible |

---

## 2. Refund Processing Timelines

| Payment Method | Processing Time |
|---|---|
| Visa / Mastercard | 7–10 business days |
| American Express | 5–7 business days |
| Meridian Wallet | 24–48 hours |
| Bank Transfer (corporate) | 10–14 business days |
| Cash (Airport Ticket Office) | Same-day cash or 10–14 days by cheque |
| Meridian Miles Redemption | 48–72 hours (miles re-credited to MA-XXXXXXXX account) |

Processing times are measured from the date Meridian Airlines confirms the cancellation.

---

## 3. Partial Refunds

### 3.1 Calculating the Partial Refund Amount

```
Refund Amount = Ticket Base Fare
              - Applicable Cancellation Fee
              + Refundable Taxes and Government Fees
              - Non-Refundable Surcharges (Economy Saver only)
```

### 3.2 Tax Refunds
Government-imposed taxes and airport fees are refundable on all fare classes, even if the base fare is non-refundable. Meridian Airlines refunds these automatically as part of the cancellation process.

---

## 4. Meridian Travel Credits

### 4.1 Issuance
Travel credits are issued via email within **24 hours** of cancellation confirmation. Credit code format: **MA-TC-XXXXXX** (e.g., MA-TC-XKP192).

### 4.2 Validity

| Fare Class | Credit Validity |
|---|---|
| Economy Saver | 12 months from original ticket issue date |
| Economy Flex | 18 months from original ticket issue date |
| Business | 24 months from original ticket issue date |
| First | 24 months from original ticket issue date |

### 4.3 Redemption Rules
- Applicable to base fare only, not to taxes and fees
- Non-transferable — must be used by the original passenger
- Can be split across multiple bookings (partial redemption permitted)
- Cannot be exchanged for cash or Meridian Miles
- Applicable only on Meridian Airlines-operated and marketed flights

---

## 5. Refund for Airline-Initiated Disruptions

When Meridian Airlines cancels or significantly delays a flight (see MA-DC-001):
- Full refund to original payment method within **72 hours** automatically
- No passenger action required
- Passengers who prefer rebooking must opt in via Manage Booking or Contact Centre

---

## 6. How to Check Refund Status

- **Meridian Website:** My Account → Refunds & Credits → Enter PNR (e.g., MA-XKPL92) or credit code (e.g., MA-TC-XKP192)
- **Contact Centre:** +1-800-MERIDIAN (have PNR and last name ready)
- Email updates sent at each processing stage

---

## 7. Non-Refundable Components

The following are **never refundable** regardless of fare class:
- Booking fees charged by third-party agents
- Credit card processing fees charged by the card issuer
- Service fees paid for agent-assisted bookings
- Pre-purchased seat selection fees (unless Meridian cancels the flight)
